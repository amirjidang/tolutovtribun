He is respected in the industry and by fans for his eclectic acting roles, charisma on screen, bevy of awards, and his environmental activism.

    Although a popular source of celebrity gossip and headlines, DiCaprio has preferred to lead as quiet of a life as possible away from the cameras. Still, it hasn't kept the public from being interested in who he has dated.

Despite the criticism, DiCaprio has been rumored to have started a relationship with another young model: Vittoria Ceretti.     Who Has Leonardo DiCaprio Dated?

    Leonardo DiCaprio has dated and been romantically linked with some of the planet's most famous and desirable women. It would be nearly impossible to mention them all, but some women he's romanced stand out more than others.

    For instance, DiCaprio famously dated Victoria's Secret model Miranda Kerr shortly after her breakup with ex-husband Orlando Bloom. The two stars were spotted getting cozy together at several high-profile places in Las Vegas, but eventually, the relationship fizzled out, and both were on to the next relationship.

    Before Miranda Kerr, Leonardo DiCaprio was in a whirlwind romance with actress Blake Lively. The relationship only lasted a few months, but the pair seemed inseparable as they were photographed vacationing together in various places.

    One of DiCaprio's more serious relationships was with Sports Illustrated model Bar Rafaeli. They dated for several years before they split. It was reported through various media outlets that the cause of the split was due to Leonardo DiCaprio's inability to commit long-term to the model.

    Another one of DiCaprio's more lengthy romances was with Brazilian supermodel Gisele Bündchen. At one point, things were so serious between DiCaprio and Bündchen that he brought her as a date at an Oscars ceremony.

    Fans were shocked when they split, as many thought they were the perfect couple. Years after their breakup, Bündchen finally opened up on what caused their fall-out: "When I was going through my panic attacks, I didn't even feel like I could share [them] with anyone… and the more I suppressed it, the bigger it became. I thought, if this stuff is in any way the cause of this pain in my life, it's gotta go."

    DiCaprio has also been romantically linked with musician Rihanna, model Naomi Campbell, actress Demi Moore, model Helena Christensen, and model Erin Heatherton.

    Before Vittoria Ceretti came on the scene, DiCaprio was dating another young model named Camila Morrone for quite some time before their split. Shortly after, it was widely reported in the media that DiCaprio had an on-again-off-again relationship with supermodel Gigi Hadid.

    Glamour magazine reported that a source close to Leonardo DiCaprio said that he was impressed with the model and Next In Fashion host. They added: "He really likes that she has her life together. She has a child and she's mature. He wants to be with someone with the same idea of good causes and political views as he does." A source close to Hadid also reported that she was smitten with the Oscar-winning actor.

    However, after several months of hanging out together, Gigi Hadid grew tired of her relationship with Leonardo Caprio: "Hadid is "done" with DiCaprio," claimed a source to Glamour. "She hasn't seen him for a while. She just decided that she was done with him. They are at very different places in life. She prioritizes being a mom."

    Who Is Leonardo DiCaprio's Alleged Girlfriend Vittoria Ceretti?

    Vittoria Ceretti is an Italian supermodel. Born in Brescia, Italy, to a working-class family, Ceretti had high hopes and aspirations for her life. She entered a modeling competition at the age of 14, where she became one of the finalists. The modeling competition kicked start what would be a remarkable career for Ceretti.

    The young model has appeared on the cover of countless magazines, including Vogue, Harper's Bazaar, and Glamour. Ceretti has also modeled for some of the world's premier fashion designers and houses, such as Dolce and Gabbana, Louis Vuitton, Givenchy, Versace, Yves Saint Laurent, Tom Ford, Miu Miu, and Roberto Cavalli.

    Despite her rumored romance with Leonardo DiCaprio, Vittoria Ceretti has been married to Italian DJ Matteo Milleri for several years. The pair got married in Ibiza, Spain, in what was described by spectators as a picturesque seaside wedding.

    Reports circulated that the two had become estranged a few years into their marriage. The gossip gained credence after Ceretti took to social media, where she posted a cover of herself singing to Alicia Key's ballad No One along with the caption: separation.

    It was only a short time before the media noted that neither Milleri nor Ceretti wore their wedding rings. Although the two were likely going through a separation, neither Ceretti nor Milleri have publicly confirmed a divorce.
