    Bravo's Real Housewives is stirring up a storm in Dallas as the ladies prepare for their trip on over to Oklahoma. While there has been loads of drama this season, mainly between Kary Brittingham and D'Andra Simmons, it seems as if D'Andra has now found herself on Brandi Redmond's bad side.

    Although the duo battled it out before leaving for their weekend trip, all eyes were on none other than Kameron Wescott and her brand new dream house. Despite having a rocky start with RHOD newbie, Tiffany Moon, Kameron has now moved on from her tiff with Tiff and is set to move in!

    Considering the house spans 10,000 square feet, it certainly cost a pretty penny! While we know Kameron is worth an impressive $4.5 million, just where does her husband, Court Westcott fall into place, especially after buying them their mini-mansion?

    How Much Is Court Westcott Worth?

    During tonight's episode of Real Housewives of Dallas, our favorite ladies prep for yet another cast trip, however, this time around, they're driving on up to Oklahoma!

    While the drama between D'Andra and Brandi is only just beginning, all the focus is now on Kameron and her brand new dream house. Although there was a lot of back and forth when it came to the purchase, the Westcott's managed to secure the property, and it was all thanks to Kameron's hubby, Court Westcott.

    The duo has quite a fun relationship! Not only do they appear to be complete opposites, but the two have a way to certainly keep one another on their toes, hence Court's secret surprise in tonight's episode.

    After making it seem as if the contract on Kameron's dream house fell through, Court brought her over for one final look-through before dropping the news that he secretly bought the home!

    The large purchase left many fans curious as to how much Court is worth, and well, it appears as if he's worth less than Kameron is, but not by too much. According to Exact Net Worth, Court comes in with a net worth of $2 million!

    Court comes from a very prestigious family in Dallas, Texas. Westcott's job is as the managing partner for his family's business, Westcott LLC, which was founded back in 1996 by his father, Carl Westcott.

    In addition to the family biz, Court also has a strong focus on investing in emerging tech and AI, which he does primarily with European and American companies. Although he may be worth $2 million at the moment, it goes without saying that Court has quite the empire on his hands, and will be worth much more one day!

    As if his business skills weren't enough, Court is also very intuitive. Considering his family is well-known across the state, their reputation means everything. The entrepreneur urged his wife, Kameron to stay clear of LeAnne Locken following her racist remarks, which remains one of the best bits of advice he's given on the show.
