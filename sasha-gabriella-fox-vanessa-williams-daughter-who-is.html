    Let's take a look at the amazing career that Sasha Gabriella Fox has had so far.

    A Model And Actress

    Vanessa has three children with her first husband Ramon Hervey II, and she also shares a child, Sasha Gabriella Fox, with her second husband Rick Fox, although they are no longer married.

    Sasha Gabriella Fox is a successful model and actress. According to Spock And Christine, Sasha is now 21 years old and she is represented by Natural Models LA and One Management. After studying at The Hervey School and graduating with her high school diploma, she is attending the Dolge College of Film & Arts at Champan University.

    Sasha is definitely doing really well in her modelling career and her future looks great, as she shares her photo shoots on her Instagram account, where she has over 40,700 followers. That is definitely an impressive amount and she is sure to only grow her following in the coming years. She also talks about the brands that she's working with.

    'One Fine Christmas'

    Everyone loves a great Christmas movie and since there are always so many new ones coming out each holiday season, fans have so many too choose from.

    Sasha Gabriella Fox is also known for starring in the 2019 TV movie One Fine Christmas. This was special as Sasha's parents Rick Fox and Vanessa Williams starred in the movie as well.

    The official description on IMDb reads, "A group of neighbors struggles with finding time for their families during the holiday season." This movie is one of several original Christmas films premiering on OWN and it definitely sounds really sweet.

    Vanessa Williams As A Mom

    Vanessa Williams has had an incredible acting career and has worked steadily for decades. TV fans will recognize the actress from playing Renee Perry on Desperate Housewives, Olivia Doran on 666 Park Avenue, and Maxine Robinson on Daytime Divas, among many other roles. Vanessa is also an accomplished singer and has released 8 albums, and she has also done a lot of theater work.

    Vanessa also was named Miss America in 1983 and according to Time, she returned the crown when she found out that Penthouse would put out some photos of her. She had been told that no one could tell that it was her in the photos when she originally took them.

    After divorcing Sasha's dad Rick Fox in 2004, Vanessa Williams married Jim Skrip in 2015. Vanessa has three children besides Sasha: Jillian Hervey, Melaine Hervey, and Devin Hervey, all from her first marriage to Ramon Hervey II which lasted from 1987 to 1997.

    In an interview with Mom.com, Vanessa shared that while she has been raising her kids, they have always been part of her acting career. The actress shared, "My kids are used to jumping on a plane with me and going to wherever I'm going to be set up for doing a film or on location, being on the road. My kids all have their passports; they've all been used to Mom's job taking them to places, and they're used to their life on the road. That's a part of who I am and they don't question it."
