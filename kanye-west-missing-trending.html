    Kanye West missing has been trending, but people were curious to know why this is trending. Is he really missing? Let’s find out.

    Kanye West has been gathering attention from all over the world after getting divorced from Kim Kardashian. 

    Ever since he was divorced, the 45-year-old rapper hasn’t said anything, and his fans were wondering where he is now. Has he disappeared? 

    Kanye West missing

    ‘Kanye West missing‘ is currently trending over the internet as his fans were concerned for the 45-year-old rapper after not hearing anything since his divorce. 

    Rumors were circulating over the internet that the rapper was missing. In a publication, it was written, “Kanye West has reportedly been missing and unable to find for weeks according to his ex-business manager.”

    The rumor shared through a tweet began trending within minutes after uploading. It was confirmed that the tweet gathered more than 11 million views and more than 6K tweets.

    Since the post was uploaded on Twitter, the ex-husband of Kim Kardashian has yet to address the rumor. 

    Kanye West’s ex-manager

    According to sources, it was confirmed that Kanye West’s ex-manager is Thomas St John.

    In March 2022, John was hired by Ye back, and he was reported for suing the rapper for allegedly refusing to transfer his fees. 

    Since authorities cannot locate Kanye, John is facing difficulties serving legal papers in court. There is no current address for Kanye right now. 

    Fans’ reaction to the missing rapper

    There have been several rumors circulating that the 45-year-old rapper and ex-husband of Kim Kardashian has been reported to go missing.

    A rumor was spread which said that Kanye West went missing after attending Kardashian’s 2022 Christmas party.

    A user went on social media to write, “Is Kanye really missing??? If this is true we need to find him.” Another user wrote in their post, “Kanye West has gone missing?! I just hope he’s okay and in a safe space.”
