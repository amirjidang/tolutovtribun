    Sean Connery's $33.9 Million Villa Can Be Bought By James Bond Fans

    Fans obsessed with James Bond will now have a chance to live in the same house that their titular hero called home as Sean Connery's luxurious French mansion can be purchased for the low, low price of only...$33.9 million.

    14 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Fans obsessed with James Bond will now have a chance to live in the same house that their titular hero called home as Sean Connery's luxurious French mansion can be purchased for the low, low price of only...$33.9 million.

    Maybe "low, low" is in the eye of the beholder, but Connery's estate is still on the market and with fans dashing to see the pictures used to entice buyers, 007's former residence is generating a ton of buzz as it leaves die-hards shaken, not stirred.

    RELATED: Sean Connery Voted As Best James Bond In Radio Times Poll

    Connery sunk a large amount of his massive wealth into his many homes and, with an eye for affluence, he has found himself the owner of many enviable houses. This particular mansion is known as La Roc Fleuri (The Flowered Rock) in the French Riviera. If the fact that it has a name doesn't tip one off to its over-the-top opulence, then the layout will.

    The gorgeous villa is not only breathtaking on a level most never encouter, but it is the snapshot of natural beauty. Covered in vines of wisteria and overlooking Nice, the windows show a view unlike any other. With over 10,000-square feet of pure comfort and amenities, Sean's former home features an indoor pool, a gym, spa, and and unbeatable kitchen. Whatever is cooked inside can be enjoyed outside as an outdoor dining terrace gives both residents and guests a chance to dine while overlookingthe Mediterranean Sea. Suddenly, that $33.9 million is starting to make more and more sense.

    The home was originally purchased by Connery following his 1970s marriage to Micheline Roquebrune in the 1970s. The pair, according to People, occupied the estate for close to 12 years and remained together until Sean's passing on Halloween 2020.

    At the time of his passing, Connery's net worth was around $350 million. His film roles, while most notably James Bond, included many unforgettable parts including cinematic classics, like Indiana Jones and the Last Crusade. Many have speculated that the poor reception lead to his retirement in 2006.

    A representative for Knight Frank real estate consultants told CNN that La Roc Fleuri has been on the market since before Halloween last year and is still available for purchase.
