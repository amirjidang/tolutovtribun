    British musician and actor Harry Styles has dated many famous women over the years. While the former One-Direction singer has broken many hearts in Hollywood, his legions of adoring fans hope his latest rumored girlfriend, Canadian-born actress Taylor Russell, is the one.

    What Is Taylor Russell's Net Worth And How Does She Spend Her Money?

    Actress Taylor Russell has undoubtedly gained the respect of the entertainment industry after starring in many critically acclaimed works. And while she has certainly garnered a lot of fans thanks to her talent, charm, and personality, interest in the actress has risen steadily since she was romantically linked first with actor Timothee Chalamet and, more recently, with music superstar Harry Styles.

    This has inadvertently led fans to wonder many things about the notoriously private actress, two of which are: how much is Taylor Russell's net worth, and what does she spend her money on?

    While many reports vary on wealth, several prominent websites estimate that Taylor Russell's net worth is anywhere from $5 million to $10 million; this is, of course, thanks partly to the many successful TV shows and films she has starred in.

    The actress is also the brand ambassador for several high-end brands, such as the Spanish luxury fashion house Loewe.

    While Taylor Russell enjoys jet-setting across luxurious destinations such as London and wears the best fashion money can buy, her childhood years were quite different from her current glamorous lifestyle.
