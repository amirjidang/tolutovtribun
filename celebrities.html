    About

    Brie Bella worked as a model and actor before landing a role with the WWE. In fact, she and twin sister Nikki Bella took part in the WWE Diva Search in 2006 but didn’t make the cut. However, WWE signed the pair in June 2007 and they made their in-ring debut as The Bella Twins in September of that year. She remained a wrestler until March 2019, when she announced she was officially retired. The sisters are set to be inducted into the WWE Hall of Fame in 2021 and star in their own E! reality show, Total Bellas.

    In addition to wrestling, Brie and Nikki have a wine line called Belle Radici, a lifestyle line called Birdiebee and a beauty line called Nicole + Brizee.

    Brie married wrestler Bryan Danielson in April 2014. The couple welcomed daughter Birdie in May 2017 and a son, Buddy, in August 2020.
