    Rowan Atkinson is the fabulously funny actor and brilliant mastermind behind Mr. Bean. His familiar, expressive face is one that millions of fans across the globe recognize fondly as being part of their most beloved television memories. As one of the most highly esteemed actors originating from the U.K., Atkinson brought light-hearted humor to the living rooms of his fans and quickly became part of the comedic landscape that so many people relied upon.  In no time at all, Atkinson's image became synonymous with his Mini Cooper, as he zipped his little car back and forth in nearly every episode. In real life, however, Rowan Atkinson's car dreams far surpassed the adorable Mini Cooper. In fact, he was quite the motorhead and he acquired an astounding car collection that would make any auto enthusiast green with envy. Quickly amassing a vehicular collection that's worth well over $15,000,000, Atkinson's obsession with automobiles makes his garage one that fans dream of peering into.

     Audi A8 - $100,000

    No car collection is complete without the prestigious 4-ringed decal belonging to the Audi A8. This agile, sleek car packs nearly 500 horsepower under a very stylish hood, and it's easy to see why Atkinson would insist on including one in his car collection. His A8 easily rivals other vehicles with its superior handling, and unique ability to hug snug corners with precision and ease.

     Jaguar MK7 - $30,000

    The Mark VII was a luxury car built from 1951 to 1956. This vehicle effortlessly clocked up to 100 mph, and as a result, it quickly became a favorite on the track. Perfectly designed to race and rally, this car is a peppy beauty that is a prized addition to any automobile collection, and a pure joy to blast at full speed, which Atkinson did at every opportunity.

    Land Rover Defender Heritage - $232,000

    According to Auto Express, the Land Rover Defender Heritage is a go-anywhere, do-anything vehicle that is built to be rugged and durable. Designed in limited quantities, "only 400 Heritage models will be sold in the UK, available in this short-wheelbase 90 bodystyle or long-wheelbase 110, and all are finished in distinctive Grasmere Green metallic paint with a contrasting white roof," making this a fun, valuable addition to Atkinson's garage.

    2011 Rolls Royce Phantom Drophead - $500,000

    Via: WikiCommons

    A Rolls Royce is a must-have for any car enthusiast, and Rowan Atkinson wasn't about to settle for just any model. Dropping an astounding $500,000 on a Rolls Royce Phantom Drophead, the actor is a proud owner of this sleek, luxurious craftsmanship that stands alone in a class of sheer opulence and power. Atkinson customized his 2011 Drophead by having a 9 liter V16 engine installed, adding more 'oomph' to a car that already boasts hand-painted crests and opulent detailing.

     2014 Bentley Mulsanne Birkin - $400,000

    Atkinson's 2014 Bentley Mulsanne Birkin edition is the ultimate in lavishly designed comfort and sleek vehicular styling. Exuding opulence with every curve, this impeccably designed car is a limited edition masterpiece that is a true leader in the luxury sedan class. Premium features are found within this vehicle,  inclusive of of twin 8-Inch LCD screens installed in the headrests of the rear seats and a highly enhanced audio system.  Refined elegance as extravagant as this has set Atkinson back an astounding $400,000.

    Rolls Royce Ghost - $500,000

    The perfectly crafted Rolls Royce Ghost comes with a whopping $500,000 price tag, which is no match for Rowan Atkinson's $150 million fortune. He splurged on this car without hesitation, adding yet another finely crafted machine to his fleet of superior automobiles. Atkinson's Ghost is powered by a 563 horsepower 6.7-liter V-12 engine that purrs powerfully and effortlessly oozes class and luxury.

    BMW 328 Roadster - $1,200,000

    This sheer classic is a very special car that adds a whole lot of character to Rowan Atkinson's car collection. One of his most prized possessions, this rare car was only created between 1936-1940, and came with a standard 4 speed manual transmission. Given the rare nature of this car, it's assumed that Atkinson keeps it stored for special occasions, as parts for this beauty would not only be expensive, but also very hard to come by.

    Mercedes Benz SLS AMG - $260,000

    Power and pedigree collide with the stunning Mercedes Benz SLS AMG. Only the most sophisticated car shoppers would be able to afford the price tag on this beautiful car, and the sleek performance features and quick responsiveness of the engine certainly make it a highly enjoyable car to drive.

    1997 Mclaren F1 - $12,000,000

    One of the most iconic supercars to ever be built happens to be sitting in Rowan Atkinson's garage. The 1997 Mclaren F1 is a high powered, ultra styled machine that is fully loaded with every imaginable feature and powered by BMW Motorsports. This incredible car boasts a center-biased driving position and is loaded with a naturally aspirated 6.1 liter V12, making it an absolute powerhouse that can compete with any other car in its class.

    1986 Aston Martin V8 Zagato, $670,000

    This Italian-made masterpiece is powered by a 5.3 liter V8 engine that churns out an impressive 430 horsepower, and tops out at 185 mph. Atkinson was one of the few that owns this collector's car, and one of even fewer people around the world who was forced to completely rebuild it.  This prized possession fell victim of a crash during one of Rowan's circuit racing events, and the damage to his car left him feeling absolutely devastated. He ended up spending upwards of $300,000 to rebuild the car, so that he could re-add it to his car collection.
