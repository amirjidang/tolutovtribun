    Lala Kent rose to fame on The Vanderpump Rules, but she met Randall Emmett well before he started to appear on the show. Their relationship began when Lala worked at Lisa Vanderpump's restaurant, SUR in 2015. Randall was hosting his Christmas party there and had just separated from his wife at the time. He requested Lala's number, and the two became intimate on their very first date, with Lala claiming she knew right away she would be with Randall 'forever.'

    The relationship between the two was full of ups and downs, which made them a super hot tabloid item. They were always surrounded by drama, and the world loved watching it all unfold. Lately, fans have learned there were many more layers to the relationship that have just now begun to surface...

    10 They Were Engaged To Be Married

    In September of 2018, this hot couple took their already steamy relationship to the next level by getting engaged to be married, and they set their date for April of 2020. They seemed truly smitten with one another, and it seemed that their relationship was headed in the right direction - the happily ever after that Lala had always imagined would be their final destination.

    RELATED: ‘Vanderpump Rules’: Ariana Madix Slams Lala Kent For Posting A Photo With Stassi And Kristen

    9 The Pandemic Pushed Their Wedding Date

    Just as Lala and Randall neared their big day, the global pandemic hit, and like so many other couples around the world, they were forced to push their wedding to a future date. Of course, plans were already in place and excitement had already built, but everything had to be put on ice without a firm sense as to when the world would open up again and they could resume their lavish wedding plans.

    8 Their Daughter Was Born

    Their increased time together during quarantine clearly had Lala and Randall filing their time with fun, as before long, they announced that Lala was pregnant. Fans were thrilled to hear the news, and Lala beamed with joy when their little girl was born in March of 2021, and they named their gorgeous daughter 'Ocean.' Lala immediately began documenting Ocean's life on Instagram and was quick to state that she was wanting another baby right away. Randall seemed a bit less enthused about that idea.

    7 Lala Scratched All Images Of Randall From Instagram

    Everything seemed to be going well with the young family until October of 2021, when Lala suddenly deleted any and all signs of Randall from her Instagram page. Her sudden desire to scratch out all references of him was a big sign to fans that there was trouble in paradise. Rumors began to fly that Lala and Randall had broken up, and they'd soon be reinforced by a series of other abrupt changes.

    RELATED:'I Didn't Grow Up Like This': Lala Kent Claps Back At Mom-Shaming Troll

    6 It Was Revealed Randall Emmett Cheated In Nashville

    It didn't take long for sources to reveal that she had reason to believe that Randall Emmett had cheated on Lala Kent in Nashville. Their picture-perfect life was suddenly exhibiting real signs of being in trouble, and of course, the saddest part was that they had a very young daughter that was now thrown into the mix. Fans were glued to any signs of reconciliation but were instead met with increased signs that would suggest the breakup was serious and was likely to be a permanent one.

    5 Lala Checks Into A Hotel

    It wasn't long before Lala Kent posted a video of herself that was far different than any others she had posted in the past. This time, the video was of her and Ocean checking in to a Beverly Hills hotel, and it was filmed as what appeared to be an intentional dig at Randall, putting him on official notice that she was dedicated to moving on, no matter what the sacrifice. She appears to have moved into the hotel for a short-term stay. Lala took the time to set this video of her hotel-move to Beyonce's song, Sorry.

    RELATED:20 Things Vanderpump Rules' Lala Kent Wishes She Could Sweep Under The Rug

    4 Lala Was Spotted Without Her Ring

    Page Six reports that a Los Angeles Barnes & Noble was the setting for Lala Kent's recent book signing, and she made more than just an appearance... she also made a statement. Lala arrived to sign autographs for eager fans that were snatching up her new memoir titled Give Them Lala. Fans couldn't help but notice that Lala wasn't wearing her engagement ring at the time, and when she was asked how she was doing, her response was; "I'm doing the best I can."

    3 Randall Emmett's Double Life Was Exposed

    There's a source that has come to the surface to leak information about Randall, and they've revealed that he has led a double life for a very long time. It is said that he has mastered the art of living as a loyal, loving significant other at times, but there are other moments that he acts quite differently. It's believed that Randall would also go on a series of benders and was very friendly with the ladies. This was proven true when he was spotted going into the Thompson hotel with two ladies in Nashville.

    2 They Bond With Their Baby .... Separately

    Ocean remains the focus for both her mom and dad, despite the fact that they are just not getting along with one another at this current time. Lala and Randall remain completely devoted to giving Ocean the very best life she can possibly have and have been sharing images of their time with her online... separately.

    The two seem to have come to some amicable co-parenting terms, as it's clear that time with Ocean is a priority for them both, and they each take advantage of the one-on-one time they have with her.

    1 Lala Kent Hints That Secrets Will Be Revealed

    The ups and downs of this relationship are a lot to take in, and it seems that there's even more dirt that will soon be dished. When asked about their relationship, Lala revealed that there was more to the story than meets the eye, and she urged fans to watch the rest of The Vanderpump Rules before they pass judgement.

    She says, “I feel like as time goes on, you’ll see him more and maybe you’ll think to yourself, ‘OK, this does make sense.’ At least that’s my hope for viewers...they finally get to dive into what our relationship is about.”
