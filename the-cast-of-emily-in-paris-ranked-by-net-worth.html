    Ever since Emily In Paris was released on Netflix in October 2020, the show has become an obsession for drama and fashion lovers worldwide. Created by Darren Star, the man behind hit shows such as Sex And The City, 90210, Melrose Place, and Younger, Emily In Paris is a story about a young woman named Emily portrayed by Lily Collins. She receives the opportunity of a lifetime when she is offered her dream job in Paris. The story progresses through Emily’s eyes as she offers an American perspective about the city of Paris and deals with her personal and professional issues.

    Starring a combination of French and American actors, Emily In Paris has a diverse cast, each having a quirky personality on the show that truly adds to the beauty of the backdrop. Coming from well-known acting backgrounds, the cast has a hefty net worth through their work over the years. Let’s take a look at some of the highest-earning cast on the show, ranked by their net worth.

    Samuel Arnold ($1 million)

    A French actor and dancer, Samuel Arnold, portrays Julien, Emily’s co-worker who has a clashing relationship with the lead during the initial episodes but later becomes her friend to keep her updated about the office gossip. According to Cosmopolitan, the role was a breakthrough role of Arnold, whose previous onscreen credits only included French television shows. The actor has a net worth of $1 million.

    Camille Razat ($1 million)

    Hailing from France, Camille Razat has already been a popular name as an actor and model. She has appeared in the French/Belgian horror movie Girls With Balls and French miniseries The Disappearance. It portrays the character of Camille, a smart and sophisticated socialite and the daughter of a business mogul who becomes one of Emily’s closest confidants and helps her discover Paris. The 27-year old has a net worth of $1 million through her acting credits.

    Philippine Leroy-Beaulieu ($1 million)

    Born and raised in Italy, Philippine Leroy-Beaulieu comes from an acting background similar to her co-star Lily Collins. Leroy-Beaulieu’s father is Philippe Leroy, an acclaimed French actor who has worked in over 150 films throughout his career. Philippine Leroy-Beaulieu has worked in several popular French movies, including Natalia and Three Men And A Cradle, earning a $1 million net worth. She has also appeared in the famous French show Call My Agent! as Catherine Barneville. She plays the character of Sylvie, Emily’s boss in the show.

    Lucas Bravo ($1.5 million)

    Playing a warm, caring French chef, Lucas Bravo became a heartthrob after his portrayal of Gabriel in Emily In Paris. Bravo’s character is also Emily’s downstairs neighbor, who is always amused with Emily’s quirky behavior and begins dating her as the story progresses. The actor has amassed a $1.5 million net worth starring in film shorts such as Beautiful Injuries and Caprice.

    Lucien Laviscount ($7 million)

    Lucien Laviscount brought British sophistication and glamor to the second season of Emily In Paris as Alfie, a London native who becomes Emily’s love interest, as stated by The Things. A UK-native, Laviscount has starred in numerous popular soap operas that have earned him a $7 million net worth. His notable television shows include Coronation Street, Waterloo Road, and Still Star-Crossed.

    Ashley Park ($9 million)

    Emily’s best friend in Paris, Ashley Park, portrays the character of Mindy Chen, an aspiring singer and nanny who works for the elite families in Paris. She becomes close friends with Emily after bonding over their similar thoughts about American and French culture. Ashley Park has amassed a $9 million fortune from her work in musicals. She is best known for starring in the Mean Girls musical as Gretchen Wieners, earning her a Tony Award nomination.

    Kate Walsh ($20 million)

    The Director of Marketing, Madeline Wheeler, allows Emily to work in Paris. Kate Walsh plays a mentor to the lead character in the show. Walsh has worked in numerous television shows and movies, amassing a $20 million net worth. She is best known to play the character of Dr. Addison Montgomery in the drama show Grey’s Anatomy.

    Lily Collins ($25 million)

    The British-American actress who is the central character of the show, Lily Collins, portrays the titular role of the ambitious marketing executive Emily Cooper in Emily In Paris. Collins has had a prolific career as a movie actress while simultaneously working in television shows. She is best known for her roles in the movies Love, Rosie, Mirror Mirror, To The Bone, The Mortal Instruments: City Of Bones, Rules Don’t Apply, and many more. As mentioned by Celebrity Net Worth, Collins has amassed a $25 million fortune through her acting career.

    After the second season's release during the holidays in 2021, Emily In Paris saw an increase in its fanbase as people binge-watched the show. As the second season ends on a cliffhanger, fans are left wanting to find out what happens next. In January 2022, Netflix announced that the show was renewed for a third and fourth season.
