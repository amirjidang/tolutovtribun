    How Did Tyler Christopher Pass Away?

    A close friend of the late “General Hospital” actor, Tyler Christopher, discovered his lifeless body at his home after becoming concerned about not hearing from him.

    Law enforcement sources in San Diego disclosed that they received a 911 call from Christopher’s apartment at approximately 9:30 a.m. on a Tuesday.

    This concerned friend, who had a spare key to Christopher’s apartment, gained access and tragically found the 50-year-old “Ice Storm” star deceased in the bedroom.

    Maurice Benard, Tyler Christopher’s co-star from “GH,” later confirmed the Emmy-winning actor’s passing on Instagram.

    Benard expressed deep sadness at Tyler’s passing, revealing that he had suffered a cardiac event in his San Diego apartment.

    He praised Christopher’s immense talent and his dedication to bringing joy to his fans through acting.

    Benard also touched on Tyler’s advocacy for improved mental health and substance use treatment, acknowledging his struggles with bipolar depression and alcohol.

    Christopher’s representative, Chi Muoi Lo, also confirmed the actor’s death and expressed shock and devastation at the loss of his actor friend.

    He extended his condolences to Christopher’s friends and family who held him in high regard.
