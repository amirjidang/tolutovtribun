    Angelina Jolie debuted a dramatic transformation this week following reports that her ex-husband Brad Pitt is getting serious with Ines de Ramon. So much so, it sounds like the actor is hoping to expand his family with his girlfriend.

    Angelina made a recent appearance at her Atelier Jolie boutique located in New York City, where she showcased her freshly dyed blonde hair. The 48-year-old actress looked elegant in an all-black ensemble featuring a jacket, bootcut pants, and a white t-shirt. To complete her look, Angelina accessorized with tinted black sunglasses and carried a themed black bag inspired by her iconic role in Girl, Interrupted.

    Angelina Changed Her Hair While Working On A Big Project With Her Kids

    Angelina’s recent sighting appears to have been a work outing. She’s been working on launching the fashion house Atelier Jolie since last year, even crediting several of the children she shares with Brad Pitt for helping her bring the vision to life.

    “I don’t want to be a big fashion designer. I want to build a house for other people to become that,” Angelina told Vogue about the physical space. “I’ve met a lot of artisans over the years—very capable, talented people—and I’d like to see them grow.”

    She said it was her 19-year old son Pax who helped design the brand’s logo, while her eldest daughter, Zahara, 18, was a key player in helping her choose the New York space.

    “I can be very impulsive, but Zahara is so grounded, decisive, and thoughtful,” the Maleficent said. “When she agreed, I felt we were both decided.”

    While Angelina clearly has a close bond with her kids, reports say Brad’s relationship has been strained with them since their 2016 divorce. Recent reports have said that while he’s hopeful about repairing the bond, he’s also focusing on the future, including he what he can create with his current girlfriend, Ines.

    Brad Reportedly Wants To Have Children With Ines De Ramon

    The couple have been relatively private about their relationship, but reports say Ines and Brad have discussed marriage and children.

    “Brad and Ines are smitten with each other and she recently moved into his Los Feliz mansion, proving how serious they are,” an insider told Closer Magazine. “He’s known from the outset that Ines wants to become a mum and that’s something they discussed very early on, given their 30-year age gap.”

    They noted that Ines was initially “surprised” by Brad’s “willingness to become a dad again, but he insisted it was something he very much wants too.”
