    Southern Charm star Kathryn Dennis is over the moon as she always shares on social media her “low drama” relationship with boyfriend Chleb Ravenell. But who really is this man of her life? Anyone who isn’t from the south will be able to get a grasp of who is and what he does. Here’s what we’ve found out!

    Who Is Chleb Ravenell?

    Fans who have been following Kathryn will probably remember her ex-husband’s name, Thomas Ravenel, and some might have noticed the similarities between her new love and former partner’s name; however, both Chleb and Thomas are not related in any way.

    Though both share identical last names, it appears Kathryn is so in love with her man. In October, she shared a photo of her hugging Chleb on Instagram with the caption “YOU.” One of the fans commented on the post, “The last name thing is uncomfortable but you go girl.”

    More than anything else, Chleb is someone who girls will admire and look up to. He was born and raised in South Carolina. He graduated from Western Michigan University with a bachelor’s degree in communication and media studies, and during his stay in college, he became a dedicated full-time student football athlete. But prior to his admission to Western Michigan, he earned an associate’s degree from George Military College.

    What Does Kathryn’s Boyfriend Do?

    It is no secret that Kathryn, who currently has a net worth of $800,000, has been living in the limelight for years and a life full of finer things, so people start to wonder what her current partner does for a living. Based on his LinkedIn profile, Chleb is a product specialist at Apple.

    Aside from work, the 32-year-old athlete appears to love traveling as seen in his Instagram posts. He has been to different places both in and outside of the United States. His social media followers can also notice that Chleb spends a lot of time working out, which is a great way to relieve stress.

    What Are the Future Plans of Kathryn and Chleb?

    Kathryn, who ended her serious relationship with Thomas, told in an interview with PEOPLE that she and her new beau are “building a life together.” Though she admitted that they haven’t said the word marriage yet, they have discussed long-term plans, adding that he is more than her previous relationships ever.

    The 29-year-old beauty also reaffirmed that Ravenell is really great with her kids, Kensie, 6, and son Saint, 4, both of whom she shares with ex-husband Thomas. Besides her admiration for her new lover, she admitted that they are in an “adult relationship” and she really does feel like she has a partner.

    In an Instagram post of the South Carolina native, he was seen with Kathryn and her daughter. The caption read, “these girls can EAT!” The photo that showcased the threesome together with Chleb's mother proved that he has already introduced his girlfriend to his family.
