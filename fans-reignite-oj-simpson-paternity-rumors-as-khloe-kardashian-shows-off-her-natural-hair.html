    Khloe Kardashian has left a lot of people talking on social media after posting a photo of herself flaunting her “natural hair” in one of her latest Instagram posts.

    The mother of one shared a mid-workout pic with her 174 million followers, while her caption read, “I rarely wear my natural hair texture. Felt kinda cute with it (please don’t ruin the feeling).”

    Well, it appears Khloe definitely didn’t get the response she was looking for, as her name began to trend on Twitter, with many choosing to reignite some unsavory paternity rumors surrounding her, and claiming that there’s no way the Good American founder shared any resemblance to the late Robert Kardashian.

    RELATED: Khloe Kardashian Fans Want Her To Quit Getting This One Cosmetic Treatment (For True's Sake)

    It’s long been alleged that Kris Jenner had shared a fling with O.J. Simpson back in the ‘80s, though the famous momager has heavily denied these claims in the past — still, they’ve led a lot of fans to question whether "KoKo" is actually a Kardashian.

    Social media users were quick to pull up throwback photos of the 37-year-old standing next to her sisters Kim and Kourtney Kardashian when they were still children, asking others to weigh in on whether she looked anything like her siblings going up.

    Most people agreed, saying that Kourtney's appearance was a striking contrast to sisters Kim and Kourtney. Given that, and previous claims from Robert Kardashian’s ex-wife Ellen Pierson, alleging that the former attorney knew Khloe wasn’t his daughter, a lot of people have been left speculating about the decades-old rumor.

    RELATED: The Next Generation Of Kardashians Treated To Ice Cream As Fans Adore Them

    Simpson previously addressed the media controversy in a video he posted to his Twitter in 2019, stressing that while he once shared a close friendship with Jenner and her ex-husband, they were never romantic with one another.

    "Bob Kardashian, he was like a brother to me. He was a great guy,” he said. "He met and married Kris and they really had a terrific time together, when they were together. Unfortunately, that ended.

    “But never, and I want to stress never, in any way shape or form, have I ever had any interest in Kris romantically or sexually. And I never got any indication that she ever had any interest in me. So all of these stories are just bogus. Bad. Tasteless.”

    Jenner herself has also shut down these claims in the past, saying that Simpson was a close friend during her marriage with Kardashian, which fizzled out after the death of her best friend — and Simpson’s ex-wife — Nicole Brown — in June 1994.
