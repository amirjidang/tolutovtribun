    The Early Life Of Julia Garner

    Garner was the daughter of the painter Thomas Garner, an art teacher from Shaker Heights in Ohio. She was born on February 1, 1994, in Riverdale, a borough in The Bronx, New York City. Julia Garner is Jewish as her mother, Tami Gingold. Her mother is a therapist and comedian actress in her native land, Israel. As a child, she watched classic movies with her parents, big movie fans like herself. Julia might have emerged from a classic film with her almond shape, navy blue eyes, and blond curls, making her look unique from other actors. Garner attended Eagle Hill School in Greenwich, Connecticut, and earned a psychology degree from Indiana University in 2015.

    Julia has participated in various plays while taking a workshop for acting, and her older sibling Anna Garner is a writer, producer, and artist. Garner married in December 2019 to Mark Foster, an American singer, songwriter, and the lead singer of the band, Foster The People by profession. They had no children, according to any source as of today.
