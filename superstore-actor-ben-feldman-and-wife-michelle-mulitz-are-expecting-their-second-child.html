    A subtle announcement! Superstore actor Ben Feldman took to Instagram on Tuesday, November 6, to share that he and wife Michelle Mulitz are expecting their second child. But the 38-year-old didn’t post an obvious baby bump pic. In the photo, a pregnant Mulitz sports a T-shirt that’s emblazoned with the words “Voting for 2.”

    The Silicon Valley star and Mulitz posed for the snap after voting in the Midterms on Tuesday in Los Angeles. A beaming Feldman holds their 12-month-old son, Charlie, upside down in the shot. “We voted as a family because it’s about the future of our children,” Feldman told Us Weekly in a statement. Charlie’s sibling is due to arrive in Spring 2019. 

    In May, the Mad Men alum acknowledged that fatherhood is “exhausting,” but so worth every sleepless night. “It’s really weird that you can make something from scratch and it turns out to be a human with its own personality,” he told Extra TV.

    Us Weekly exclusively confirmed that Feldman and Mulitz tied the knot in October 2013 in Gaithersburg, Maryland, after seven years of dating.
