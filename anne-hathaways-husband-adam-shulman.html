    If not on movie sets filming her next blockbuster, Anne Hathaway would be found in her home with her husband. The Princess Diaries’ actor told a friend she was going to marry Adam the instant she set her eyes on him.

    “I knew from the second I met him that he was the love of my life,” Anne Hathaway revealed and reminisced years later in an interview she gave to Harper’s BAZAAR UK. The famed actor values privacy when it comes to her innate feelings, but couldn’t hold back.

    The actor is an Oscar winner and celebrated in her industry as a gifted and talented actor. 

    Many people know that Shakespeare’s wife was also named Anne Hathaway, and they can’t stop comparing the names. Since many people also don’t know much about Anne Hathaway’s husband as well, they ponder whether he is a reincarnation of the author or not.

    But who is Anne Hathaway’s husband, Adam Shulman?

    Adam Shalman is an actor and a producer along with being a jewelry designer. He has appeared in notable films and TV series. The Dukes of Hazzard: The Beginning and American Dreams are names that most people know him by.

    He was born on 2nd April 2981. His interests have taken him to other ventures, where he likes to design jewelry. He co-owns a brand called James Banks Design which specializes in handmade jewelry. Its website places it in Northern California.

    The actor’s fairytale began when they both met in 2008 at a film festival. Mutual friends brought them together and as the actor proclaimed she was going to marry Adam, that happened a few years later.

    Anne Hathaway’s husband is the ideal one to be stranded alone with when quarantined. Anne herself revealed this when she talked about the challenges she faced in this pandemic. 

    “In the early days of lockdown we were all so overwhelmed and there was a lot to process and I needed a release but I didn’t want to scare my kids, so I screamed into a pillow a lot.”
