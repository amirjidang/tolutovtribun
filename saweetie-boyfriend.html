    Saweetie dated Quavo, the Migos band member.

    The 28-year-old rapper indicated a potential new love interest on Instagram earlier this year. 

    Saweetie posted a picture of herself kissing a man’s forehead, but the male had his back to the camera. 

    She included a shushing emoji in the photo’s text. Later, she shared a picture of a man who appeared to be different. 

    The image showed a shirtless man encircling a Saweetie wearing a tank suit; it was edited so that his face was hidden. 

    She later dispelled rumors by announcing that the pictures were for her brand-new music video for the song “Closer” with H.E.R.

    Although the musician hasn’t addressed the rumors or spoken publicly about her current love interests, fans presume Saweetie has a partner.

    The Three-Year Relationship Between Saweetie and Quavo

    Quavo, one of the band members of Migos, and Saweetie dated for three years before calling it quit in March 2021. 

    In a string of tweets, Saweetie seemed to imply that Quavo had been unfaithful. 

    Quavo earlier disclosed that he jumped into Saweetie’s Instagram D.Ms in March 2018 to get a chance to know her while referring to her smash song “ICY GRL.”

    Soon after, the couple began dating in 2018. 

    The couple was renowned for lavishly showering each other with presents and was frequently spotted cuddling up to one another on red carpets.

    For Christmas in December 2020, Saweetie gave Quavo a $300,000 rare Richard Millie watch. Quavo gifted his girl a brand-new white Bentley.

    Three years later, the couple parted ways after getting into a physical argument in an elevator sometime in 2020, and the couple formally split in March 2021. 

    Saweetie announced her single status to the world on Twitter.

    Rumors of a rekindled romance between Quavo of the Migos and her spread after she publicly announced their breakup. 

    The media and fans have decided that the split was acrimonious and ugly.

    The Best Friend singer ultimately broke down after saying she was frustrated and couldn’t keep up with the lies on numerous occasions.

    Relationship History 

    Following her split with Quavo, the rapper was said to have dated Roddy Ricch after being spotted sitting next to him at a Los Angeles Lakers basketball game in October 2021, which she denied.

    She was rumored to be dating Lil’ Baby in November 2021, but she denied it.

    Another rumor circulated about the dating rumors of Saweetie and Damson Idris in November 2021 after he posted a video of Saweetie playing the piano for him. 

    In a recent interview with GQ, Idris dispelled the dating rumors by clarifying that they are simply friends and that he is single.

    Saweetie also dated Justin Combs in 2016 for some time. However, the couple parted ways shortly. The reason behind the breakup is not known.

    Keith Powers and Saweetie reportedly dated for four years. But in 2014, the pair decided to end their relationship. 

    In an interview with “Hollywood Unlocked,” Powers said that he dated Saweetie for four years between the ages of 18 and 22. Keith also stated that their relationship was just too toxic.
