    'Fate: The Winx Saga': Who Is Abigail Cowen?

    From creator Brian Young from The Vampire Diaries, Fate: The Winx Saga is a live-action reimagining of the Italian cartoon Winx Club from Iginio Straffi.

    Abigail Cowen is an up-and-coming actress, and she's gaining a bigger fan base after starring in big shows like Chilling Adventures of Sabrina and Fate: The Winx Saga.

    A Talented Musician

    The Netflix star is 22 years old, and her birthday is on March 18, 1998. Abigail was born in Gainesville, Florida, and raised on a farm there.

    From Instagram, fans can see that she's not just a great actress, but she's also a fantastic musician. Abigail sings and she plays the guitar and the piano. The actress even shared a video of her singing while Gavin Leatherwood plays the guitar.

    Abigail Has Appeared in Some of Your Favorite Shows and Movies

    If you think she looks familiar, that's because she stars in a few very well-known Netflix shows. She stars as Bloom in Fate: The Winx Saga, Dorcas in Chilling Adventures of Sabrina, and she's in a few episodes of Stranger Things as the popular girl Vicky.

    Related: How Does Melissa Joan Hart Feel About ‘The Chilling Adventures Of Sabrina’?

    The actress is also in a few episodes of The Power Couple, The Fosters, Wisdom of the Crowd, and Red Band Society. She even appears in the movie I Still Believe alongside KJ Apa and Britt Robertson.

    Abigail Became Best Friends With Most of Her Co-Stars

    She's worked with her co-stars from Chilling Adventures of Sabrina for many years, so it's no surprise that she's very close with most of them. The actress shared many photos and videos hanging with the cast off set like Gavin Leatherwood, Ross Lynch, Tati Gabrielle, Adeline Rudolph, and Chance Perdomo. But she also has a close friendship with Outer Banks' star Madelyn Cline after the two appeared together in Stranger Things, they had just met, but they instantly connected and decided to be roommates in Los Angeles.

    Her latest project is Fate: The Winx Saga, and she's already shared many photos whit their co-stars off-screen, like Hannah van der Westhuizen, the actress that plays Stella.

    Abigail Had a Passion For Acting When She Was Young

    When she was in grade 4, she told her mom that she wanted to be an actress they signed her up for acting classes. She went to Oviedo High School, where she did track and field, and she studied public relations at the University of Florida. Then, she moved to LA with her family to pursue her acting career.

    Fate: The Winx Saga Cast

    Fate: The Winx Saga is Netflix's new adaptation of the animated series, The Winx Club. The series is a teen fantasy that follows Bloom's life in the Otherworld, and it features a set of new actresses and actors.

    Although this series isn't the only Netflix show in which you've seen Abigail Cowen, playing Bloom is her biggest role to date. How about the other cast members?

    Hannah van der Westhuizen as Stella: Hannah is 25 years old, and she was born in London, England. Fate: The Winx Saga is her big breakout role. Before that, she's only had minor appearances in movies like Get Lost! and The Bay of Silence.

    From Instagram, it seems like she's not dating anyone at the moment. Hannah is very close with her co-stars. She shared many photos hanging out with the whole cast, like Abigail Cowen and Precious Mustapha.

    Danny Griffin as Sky: Danny is 23 years old, and he was born in London and raised in Cornwall, England. Before starring as Sky, starred in the teen series Get Even, and he's in one episode of Free Rein.

    Elisha Applebaum as Musa: Elisha is 25 years old, and she was born in Watford, England. She got her big break from Fate: The Winx Saga. Before that, she only appeared in short films and lesser-known movies. But acting isn't all she does. Elisha also writes poetry and loves photography.

    Precious Mustapha as Aisha: Precious is 23 years old and was born in London, England. She's only in a couple of episodes of Code 404, The Stranger, and Endeavour, so starring in Fate: The Winx Saga is her most prominent role. She's become good friends with Hannah.

    Eliot Salt as Terra: Eliot is 26 years old and was born in Stockport, England. She appears in the series Normal People, Intelligence, and Game Face.

    Sadie Coverall as Beatrix: Sadie is 18 years old. She's a fairly young and new actress, so apart from starring as Beatrix, her only other acting credit is in the movie Rose Plays Julie.

    Although Abigail Cowen has more experience than her co-stars, she's very kind and humble with her fellow Fate: The Winx Saga cast members. This redheaded beauty is gathering a bunch of attention from viewers, wondering what she is going to do next.
