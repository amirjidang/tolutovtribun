    Kris Evans Wikipedia Bio

    Kris Evans, born and raised in Manchester, has always had a passion for music. To pursue music as a career, he decided to continue his studies in Manchester Music Base in 2006. There, he studied music production and sound engineering.

    In 2008, he was noticed by Nigel Martin Smith of Take That to join the Manchester-based band The Mend. The group went on to compete on Britain’s Got Talent. They competed in the final but were tragically beaten to first place by a dancing dog.

    Afterward, The Mend released their debut single, ‘Where Were You?,’ which charted in the UK Top 40 and received well by local and national radio stations, including BBC Radio 1.

    Kris released a solo track in 2016, which was his first. 

    With Unsigned Only, Kris rocky/urban tune ‘N.U.M.B,’ a fan favorite, was awarded champion in the Hip Hop / R&B category. Kris was crowned Project Aloft Star 2017 champion in 2017.

    Kris had a fantastic year in 2018, performing all across Europe and releasing three singles: Famous, What’s Luv ft. Swiss, and Against The Grain. Moreover, he was named to the Mobo Awards Unsung ‘Class of 2018’.

    Recently, he has opened up his recording studio.
