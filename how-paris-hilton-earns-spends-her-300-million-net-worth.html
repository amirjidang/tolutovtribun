    Paris Hilton is one of the most photographed celebrities in the world, and the curiosity surrounding her lifestyle is due in part to the incredible fortune she was born into. Heiress to the Hilton Hotel empire, Paris was born into the lap of luxury and her wild, larger-than-life experiences have unfolded in front of the cameras. Everything she does is of interest to her millions of fans across the globe, who are all tuned in to see what she says, buys, promotes, and of course, who she is spending her time with.

    Being the heiress to a deep, exponentially huge fortune is not the only claim that Paris has on her multi-million dollar fortune. She has launched a series of her own product lines and engages in a variety of other ventures that generate significant income streams. As quickly as she earns her money, and adds to her $300 million net worth, Paris is equally great at spending it, and she does so in some very big ways.

    10 Spends: Shopping Sprees

    Most of us can only ever dream of going on shopping sprees the way Paris Hilton does. Her limitless budget allows Paris to close down any store she pleases so that she can go on a personal shopping spree.

    Only the very best of everything will do, and Paris has been known to spend tens of thousands of dollars each time she feels like spoiling herself with something new. In fact, sources indicate she spent in excess of $5000 in less than 40 minutes during a recent trip to Australia, and that was a modest, rushed shopping day.

    9 Earns: Fragrance Sales

    One of the ways Paris Hilton pads her pockets is through the sale of her wide selection of perfumes. Her brand has created fragrances such as Electrify, Dazzle, Fairy Dust, Tease, and Siren, each with their own signature aroma, and all backed by a team of people hovering around Paris to create the perfect marketing campaigns. She promotes her brand at every opportunity and rakes in the dough.

    8 Spends: Private Jets

    When Paris Hilton travels, commercial airlines simply won't do. Paris' need for luxurious surroundings and exclusive experiences means she spends a whole lot of money on private jets. She takes advantage of the corporate jet belonging to the Hilton empire, but is also known to drop big bucks on jets, on a whim, to take her to wherever she feels like going in uber-style.

    7 Earns: Event Appearances

    When you're Paris Hilton, all you have to do to get paid is show up. Being able to attach Paris Hilton's name to a party or event is a huge deal, and her enthusiastic, stylish attendance is every promoter's dream. Paris demands anywhere from $25,000 to $100,000 just to appear at an event, with most of her appearances lasting less than an hour.

    6 Spends: Fancy Homes

    When it comes to living well, Paris Hilton definitely makes no sacrifices. She has various mansions, each of which includes the very finest in furniture, decor, and design elements. Her Beverly Hills mansion is worth $5.9 million and sprawls over 7,0o0 square feet. She also owns a penthouse in one of New York's most prestigious areas, which is valued at a jaw-dropping $4.9 million.

    5 Earns : Movies And Modeling

    Much of Paris Hilton's wealth is self-made. She has appeared in 21 movies, such as This Is Paris, House Of Wax, and The Cat In The Hat, and has a number of modeling contracts under her belt. She has walked the runway during New York Fashion Week and has modeled for Colcci. According to Paper, her modeling career never turned into a full-time gig, but she has definitely been noticed each time she's graced a stage.

    4 Spends: Fast Cars

    When it comes to her cars, Paris Hilton likes them pink! She has an impressive collection of supercars, such as a $300,000 California Spyder Ferrari, as well as a completely customized, Barbie-pink colored Bentley Continental GT. She also owns a high-powered  McLaren MP4-12 Spider and a McLaren 650 S along with a Rolls Royce Ghost, and that's just to name a few.

    3 Earns: Shoes & Clothing Sales

    Paris Hilton loves her shoes and purses, and her walk in closet is actually more like a series of rooms that are connected to one another with lavish, Old-Hollywood style decor and immense hallways. She has managed to take her passion for shoes and purses and churn it into a successful business venture by selling her own line to her millions of international fans. Her handbags and shoes are top sellers across the globe.

    2 Spends: Houses... For Her Dogs

    Paris Hilton absolutely adores her dogs, and she treats them like family. Of course, anyone in the Hilton family lives luxuriously and these pups are certainly no exception. Paris Hilton has spent a whopping $350,000 on custom dog houses for her pooches. This is the cost of a real home... for people!

    This dog mansion was erected on Paris Hilton's property and comes complete with chandeliers, air conditioning, and two floors for the ultimate in pet comfort.

    1 Earns: Inheritance

    Paris Hilton has an inheritance from her grandfather, who first started the Hilton empire, but that's not all. She also has a massive trust fund in place, which was set up by her father, Rick Hilton. Born into fame and fortune, Paris Hilton will never want for anything, and it seems that it's as easy for her to earn money as it is for her to spend it lavishly.
