    Dorit Kemsley Bio and early career

    Dorit Kemsley was born in Woodbridge, USA, on July 16, 1976. As her father, Shalon Lemel, is Israeli, she is also quite fluent in Hebrew. She grew up in Woodbridge, Connecticut until she was 19 years old and then left there to fulfill her dreams.

    Her initial dream was to travel the world, so she first moved to Italy and used her marketing degree at a global swimwear organization for about 10 years. It was only in 2009 that she finally decided to move to New York to start her own business called Dorit.

    Who is Dorit Kemsley’s husband? details of your wedding

    Dorit Kemsley has been happily married to an American businessman Paul PK Kemsley since 2015. The duo met in a bar in New York and after dating for a couple of years, they got married on March 7, 2015 and soon moved to the States. Joined.

    It didn’t take long for Paul to propose to her and make her his fiancée. The duo had a pretty hectic work schedule, so they had to squeeze and manage time for their wedding. Both Paul’s and Dorit’s parents attended the ceremony.

    The love birds were already parents to a son, Jagger Kemsley, when they married. A year later, in 2016, they welcomed a little daughter, Phoenix Kemsley. Despite this being Paul’s second marriage, the duo live happily together.
