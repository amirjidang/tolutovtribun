    Jesse Wesley Williams is an actor, director, producer, and activist from the United States. Jesse Williams is reportedly in a relationship with Ciarra Pardo.

    The couple were spotted on a date night in Los Angeles in June 2023. Ciarra is even referred to in his divorce court records as his “longtime girlfriend.”

    Ciarra is the co-founder of N4XT Experiences, according to her Instagram. The company specialises on live events for many industries such as fashion, cosmetics, and gaming.

    The corporation also owns Los Angeles Fashion Week and operates Beauty Days, an immersive beauty event. Ciarra was the Chief Creative Officer of Fenty before founding the firm.

    Jesse Williams appeared in films such as The Cabin in the Woods (2012) and The Butler (2013) as Dr. Jackson Avery in the ABC medical drama Grey’s Anatomy (2009-22).

    He was an executive producer on the Academy Award-winning short, Two Distant Strangers (2020), as well as the Tony Award-winning revival of Take Me Out (2022), for which he was nominated for Best Featured Actor in a Play.
