    Keep reading as we break down the finer points of Dave Bautista's net worth and his career since Guardians.

    9 Dave Bautista's Wrestling Career

    In 2002, Dave Bautista joined World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE). He used the name "Batista" in the ring and quickly moved up the ranks. According to WWE, this was primarily due to his 6'6" frame and 290 lbs of rock-solid muscle body. Fans loved him because of how huge, super strong, and good in the ring he was. Batista went on to win six world titles in WWE. Batista was reported to have made $813,000 per year in 2004, says Sportsmanor, and after getting a raise or two, he was earning approximately $1 million per year until 2010, which ultimately launched Dave Bautista's net worth.

    8 Dave Bautista's Transition To Acting

    Dave Bautista's transition from the wrestling ring to the big screen was not without its challenges. In the mid-2000s, he began exploring acting opportunities while still wrestling. He made his acting debut in small roles, including appearances on television shows like Smallville and Chuck. However, it wasn't until he joined the Marvel Universe that acting really made a dent in his bank account.

    7 Dave Bautista's Net Worth Grew Once He Was Cast As Drax In Guardians Of The Galaxy

    In 2014, Dave Bautista landed the role of Drax in what would turn out to be a massive Marvel franchise, Guardians of the Galaxy. Drax, a character known for his literal-mindedness and lack of understanding of metaphors, was not a traditional superhero. Of course, none of the Guardians are really traditional superheroes, but that's what makes it so great.

    Bautista's performance was a standout, blending humor, action, and emotional depth into his character. He was truly meant to play that part. The first film was not only a success, but an unanticipated one at that. Grossing over $770 million worldwide, it introduced Bautista to a global audience and opened the doors to more significant acting opportunities. He added $1.4 million to his net worth with this movie.

    6 Dave Bautista Post Guardians Of The Galaxy

    Following the success of 'Guardians of the Galaxy,' Dave Bautista's acting career gained momentum. He reprised his role as Drax in the film's sequels, Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2 (2017) and Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 3 (2023).

    As mentioned above, Bautista's involvement in the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) didn't end with the Guardians franchise. He appeared in Avengers: Infinity War (2018) and Avengers: Endgame (2019), two of the highest-grossing films of all time. According to Screen Rant, when all is said and done, Bautista earned upwards of $20 million from the MCU. Yes, you read that right, 'done.' Dave Bautista has made it very clear that he will not be returning to his role of Drax in any future movies.

    5 Dave Bautista As Glossu Rabban In Dune

    Dave Bautista plays Glossu Rabban in the 2021 movie Dune. Dune is another sci-fi space movie where the audience can see Bautista all painted up once again. Both Dave and his Guardians cast mates were excited about this film for him. The movie made about $397 million around the world, according to Numbers.

    4 Dave Bautista As Mr. Hinx In Spectre
