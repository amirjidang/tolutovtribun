    We all have our dream houses and interior design plans in case we ever get the chance to turn them into reality. However, for some celebs, this is not merely a dream. The wealthy bunch of the world has villas, farmhouses, and summer houses all over the world. If you’re lucky, you might get a peek inside your favorite celebs’ homes. Julia Roberts is one of the top actresses in Hollywood, known for her charm and amazing acting skills. She has received many awards and accolades including an Academy Award. She has worked as a producer for several films as well. If you think her acting portfolio is fascinating, then let’s take an inside look at her collection of real estate.

    You need to have an eye for profitable investments and beautiful real estate properties if you’re looking to build new homes. Julia Roberts has all of those qualities and that’s one of the reasons why so many fans look up to her. However, Julia Roberts herself is a big fan of acquiring scenic properties that are highly sought after in Hollywood.

    So where are all her real estate properties located?

    The Lavish Malibu Home

    Malibu is a pretty popular area among celebs and stars from all over the world. Many luxurious events are held in different areas of Malibu. The views are breathtaking and a vacation in Malibu can be just what the doctor ordered! Julia Robert’s Malibu home is spread over approximately 6200 square feet in area. The house has a total of five floors and yes you heard that right! There are five lavish and decorated bedrooms and four and a half bathrooms in this house.

    If this isn’t interesting enough for you, then you must know that Julia Roberts owns the house across the street as well. Her second Malibu home is just 20 minutes away from the first house and features three bedrooms with one bathroom. When the outer surroundings of the houses are so beautiful, we can only imagine what the interior is like! The houses have a vibrant color palette and the front entryways are all lined up and clean. There are hardwood floors all over the houses with sparkling clean white walls.

    Julia Roberts has a passion for interior design as well so there are custom and coffered ceilings. There are huge windows around the house so it gives off a historic charm. There are built-in shelves in the house for the elegant fireplace. There’s a second fireplace as well, situated in the dining room so it’s a perfect place to entertain your guests and have them feel cozy.

    The kitchens have custom cabinets and a cool banquette-style breakfast corner. The master bedroom is on the top floor and has a skylight window to make the view more beautiful. There is extra outdoor space so you can take enjoy the hilly side views and relax.

    A Victorian-Themed Residence

    Julia Robert’s San Francisco home was built in the early 1900s and designed by the well-known architect Sylvian Schnaittacher. The layout of the house is perfect and provides a gorgeous view of the Golden Gate Bridge and the Bay from different regions of the house. The house is Victorian-styled as it’s almost a century old and Julia Roberts bought it back in 2020. The total area that the house is built on is about one acre.

    The Presidio Heights Home

    She also owns the house across the street from this house in the luxurious Presidio Heights neighborhood. There are five bedrooms, four and a half bathrooms, two huge garages, a wine room, and a huge outdoor space with a garden. This piece of property takes you to another world because its beauty knows no bounds. The best thing about the house is that the exterior offers a completely different vibe but as soon as you step inside, there are coffered ceilings all around.

    The Kauai Homes

    Julia Robert’s explored the area of Kauai, Hawaii, and purchased a brilliant piece of property in 2011 for over 13 million dollars. This property not only had one house but there was a second house completely detached from the former. The entire estate covers about 2 acres of land. There’s a main house and the second house is referred to as the guest house. There are beautifully maintained porches at each house that offer scenic views of Hawaii.

    The main house is painted an intense shade of red and has six bedrooms and three bathrooms. This beach house has over 213 feet of beach frontage. The houses are situated in a way that gives you the perfect view of both the sea and mountains. The house has a quite spacious feel to it because of its high beamed ceilings. However, Julia Roberts sold this property in 2016 for over 28 million dollars.

    She prefers spending most of her time in her San Francisco home with her family and prefers to stay out of the public spotlight.
