    Harry Robinson Hamlin, popularly known as Harry Hamlin is an American actor, writer, and entrepreneur. He is best known for his role as Perseus in the fantasy film, Clash of the Titans. He is also known for his role as Michael Kuzak in the legal drama series, L.A. Law. He has starred in several other movies and tv shows. He was nominated for three Golden Globes for his role in L.A. Law. He also received a nomination for Primetime Emmy Award for his role in Mad Men. 

    What is Harry Hamlin Famous For?

    • His role as Perseus in the movie, Clash of the Titans.
    • His role in legal drama series, L.A. Law. 
    • His role in Mad Men. 

    Where is Harry Hamlin Born?

    Harry Hamlin was born on 30 October 1951. His birth name is Harry Robinson Hamlin. His birth place is in Pasadena, California in the United States. He holds an American nationality. He was born to a father, Chauncey Jerome Hamlin, Jr., and a mother, Bernice Robinson. He belongs to white ethnicity and his zodiac sign is Scorpio. His religion is Christianity.

    Regarding his educational background, he attended Flintridge Preparatory School and later The Hill School. He also attended classes at the Pacific Conservatory of the Performing Arts. Following his high school graduation, he attended the University of Calfornia, Berkeley. He was the president of the national fraternity Delta Kappa Epsilon in 1972. He then attended Yale University. He graduated with a dual Bachelor of Arts degree in drama and psychology in 1974. After graduating from Yale, he attended the American Conservatory Theater’s advanced actor training program. He received a Master of Fine Arts degree in acting in 1976.
