    This Week’s Raw Twitter Ratings

    According to Nielsen’s Twitter TV ratings, last night’s edition of WWE Raw finished #3 for the night. The show had a unique audience of 1.824 million, which is up from last week’s 1.270 million. The show had total impressions of 14.343 million, which is up from last week’s 8.537 million impressions.

    More Matches Revealed for WWE US Title DVD

    According to WrestlingDVDNetwork.com, the following matches will be included on WWE’s “The U.S. Championship – A Legacy of Greatness” DVD and Blu-ray that comes out in late April:

    –Ric Flair vs. Ricky Steamboat, Special Guest Referee: Andre The Giant, Mid-Atlantic September 1978
    -Barry Windham vs. Junk Yard Dog, NWA Main Event 1/29/1989
    –Sting vs. Rick Rude, WCW Clash of the Champions 17
    -Bret Hart vs. Diamond Dallas Page, WCW Nitro 10/26/1998
    –Sheamus vs. Rusev, WWE RAW 11/3/2014
