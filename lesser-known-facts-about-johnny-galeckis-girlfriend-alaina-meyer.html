    Most people are familiar with Johnny Galecki as Leonard Hofstadter, one of the nerds who starred in the American sitcom, The Big Bang Theory (TBBT). TBBT aired for a total of 12 seasons, concluding in May 2019. As usual, the cast moved on to other projects when the show wrapped up.

    Johnny has been keeping himself busy with different projects as well as spending lots of time with his new-ish girlfriend, who is now the mother of his only son. Johnny's girlfriend is named Alaina Meyer. Before she started appearing on Galecki’s arm at social events and featuring on his social media pages, we didn't know too much about her. We are not certain when the two lovebirds started their romance or exactly where and how the pair met.

    Galecki is not the most open person, especially where his private life is concerned, but after looking at some of their pics and doing a bit of digging. we unearthed some facts about the beautiful mystery woman that Galecki is head over heels for.

    15 Meyer Is From San Diego

    Meyer was just another California native until she started dating the famous Johnny Galecki. The 23-year-old was born on February 2, 1997, in San Diego, California. After finishing school, Meyer worked as a fabricator manager. Since she was little, she has been obsessed with welding.

    14 She Is A Model

    Everyone has his or her insecurities. For Galecki, it is his height. He does not like to talk about his 5’5’’ stature and when he does discuss it, he is never ecstatic about it. Interestingly, Alaina is a model. Most models are not short, which is something we are sure Galecki knew. Alaina is slightly taller than 5’5’, so she's quite petite for a model.

    13 And An Influencer

    Meyer goes by @alainamariemeyer on Instagram. She considers herself a social media influencer, which fits well with the fact that she is a model. Of course, after people found out that she was dating Galecki, her follower numbers shot up. However, her love for motorcycles (and many other fun things about the model) keep people interested in her for herself, rather than the fact that she is Galecki’s girlfriend.

    12 Meyer Is 20 Years Younger Than Galecki

    When Meyer started seeing Galecki, she was 21 years old. Galecki was 44 at the time, which puts slightly more than 20 years of age between them. This also makes Galecki twice as old as his girlfriend. Nonetheless, the couple seems happy, which proves the old saying, 'age ain’t nothing but a number'.

    11 She Loves Horses And Dogs

    One thing that is clear from Meyer’s social media pages is that the lady loves animals. She is most fascinated with horses and dogs. The animal lover plans to buy a piece of land and breed horses one day. She also likes dogs, which is clear from the different breeds of dogs she posts on Instagram.

    10 She Likes Traveling

    Which 20-something-year-old does not enjoy traveling? Definitely not Alaina Meyer! Most people in this age group are eager to explore the world and find their place, passion, or whatever it is that people are in search of out in the world. Before Galecki, she toured many places in the mainland USA.

    9 The Young Lady Adores Her Father

    Meyer has a very close relationship with her father, John Stryker Meyer. This says a lot about the kind of parent she will be to her son. John served in the U.S. Army Special Forces and has written three books that detail his experience as a Green Beret during the Vietnam War.

    8 The First Time Meyer Travelled Outside The Country Was In 2017

    The first time Meyer left the country, she went to Norway and this was just before she hooked up with Galecki. We are certain she had a good time in Norway, but she will have an even better time trotting around the globe with her baby daddy and son. The couple’s travel plans so far have mainly been within the United States.

    7 Meyer Has Been With Galecki For Almost Two Years

    It isn't known for sure when Galecki and Meyer began their romantic relationship, but people speculate that the pair must have hooked up around mid-2018. Galecki and Meyer announced their relationship in September 2018. A month later, the couple appeared together in public at the People’s Choice Awards.

    6 She Is Rumored To Be Engaged To Galecki

    In December of 2018, Galecki posted a selfie of him and his girlfriend. One thing caught the attention of his fans. He and Meyer were wearing similar bands on their left ring fingers. The couple is not legally married - this is a fact Meyer has confirmed. However, that does not mean that the pair is not considering the idea.

    5 Meyer Has A Tattoo Of Galecki’s Initials

    Meyer loves tattoos and has several on her body. One of her recent tattoos is the letters JG on her left forearm, which are Johnny Galecki’s initials. Meyer is not the only one smitten with her lover; Galecki also has the initials, AMM, inked on his body, with a heart shape below them.

    4 She Has Visited The Set Of The Big Bang Theory

    Meyer met Galecki around the time he and his cast members were winding up the filming of The Big Bang Theory. Lucky for her, she still had an opportunity to visit the set of the comedy, before things were packed up. She also got the chance to see her man in his element.

    3 Galecki’s First Photo On Her Instagram Account Was Posted In July 2018

    We still have not given up on trying to figure out exactly when these two lovebirds met. The first time Galecki appeared on Meyer’s Instagram page was in July 2018. However, the pair was in the company of other people. In August, Galecki shared a photo of himself hugging a woman who was later identified as Meyer.

    2 She Has Introduced Galecki To Her Family

    Galecki and Meyer may not be husband and wife, but they are family, especially after they welcomed a baby late last year. The Meyers may be familiar with Leonard Hofstadter, but that does not mean that they know the real Galecki, which is why Meyer plans these meetings. She wants her family to get to know her man.

    1 Meyer Will Not Be Posting Any Pics Of Her Son Anytime Soon

    Meyer and Galecki welcomed their little bundle of joy in December 2019. The baby is about 6 months old. Except for close friends and family members, the rest of the world has no idea what the little champ looks like. Meyer intends to keep it that way, to give the child some privacy.
