    Coffee is one of the most popular beverages worldwide, with over 166.63 million bags of coffee being consumed between 2020 and 2021. The consumption of coffee has increased exponentially, and there are various reasons people prefer caffeine. As the beverage taps into the dopamine stores in the brain, it emits euphoric effects that make people feel energized. Celebrities also turn to caffeine to boost their energy, and they are often spotted grabbing a cup of coffee during their morning run or while shooting for long hours on set.

    The preferences might differ for everyone; however, the caffeine intake remains a constant for every star. While some stick to their love of coffee by consuming large amounts daily, many turn it into a source of business to make profits. From Hugh Jackman and David Lynch, who started their coffee company, to Selena Gomez and David Letterman, who enjoy it daily, let’s look at the celebrities who consume a ridiculous amount of coffee in a day.

    10 Hugh Jackman

    Hailing from the land down under, where coffee is a cultural obsession, Hugh Jackman has always been addicted to caffeine. He also co-founded the Laughing Man coffee company that sells quality beans and opened cafés everywhere. Jackman calls himself a coffee snob as he is very particular about his flat white coffee, which is difficult to perfect in America.

    RELATED: Hugh Jackman Went From Being A Party Clown To Amassing A $180 Million Net Worth

    9 Ariana Grande

    Ariana Grande is a certified coffee lover, and she endorses several coffee shops and brands through social media. A fan of Starbucks, she first tweeted her love for their coffee in 2013 and has posted several photos with a Starbucks cup in hand. She consumes more than one cup of coffee a day, and her go-to drinks are a soy latte with cinnamon or a Cinnamon Cloud Macchiato, a drink Starbucks named after her.

    8 Selena Gomez

    Selena Gomez is a caffeine addict and admits to drinking several cups of coffee a day. Coffee is a tour staple for the star while touring far-off destinations and trying to curb her excitement and anxiety before a show. Gomez also ensures that she remains hydrated by drinking several glasses of water a day.

    7 David Lynch

    Legendary filmmaker David Lynch had his first taste of coffee when he was only three. His ideal cup of coffee is smooth and rich in flavor with no bitterness. He prefers to drink an espresso, latte, or cappuccino. Lynch has claimed that he used to drink twenty cups of coffee a day. He has also launched David Lynch Signature Cup coffee to share his flavor with everyone.

    RELATED: Here's Why David Lynch Isn't Interested In Seeing The 'Dune' Remake

    6 Shay Mitchell

    If it isn’t clear from Shay Mitchell’s social media and her daily café runs, she loves drinking coffee. Her usual order is a two-shot espresso that helps her remain energized throughout the day. She prefers drinking early morning coffee if her schedule starts in the early hours. She has traveled to several exotic locations and enjoyed coffee from various cultures.

    5 Machine Gun Kelly

    Machine Gun Kelly has a simple message for people in their mid-20s to have good highs during the young years of life. As pop culture has seen many icons lose their lives to drug and prescription addictions, Kelly has opened a coffee bar called the 27 Club in Cleveland, Ohio. After struggling with drug addiction, the rapper has switched to coffee and uses it to get a positive high.

    4 Taylor Swift

    Taylor Swift loves to drink several cups of coffee in a day, especially during fall, as it is her favorite season. Her go-to drink is a Starbucks grande caramel nonfat latte. In 2021 during the launch of the album Red (Taylor’s Version), Swift took her coffee love to the fans as she released a limited-edition coffee cup that had the album's theme.

    3 Jerry Seinfeld

    While Jerry Seinfeld did not understand the obsession with coffee, he finally came around to enjoying it. He even has a web series called Comedians In Cars Getting Coffee, where stars discuss their life over several cups of coffee. Seinfeld prefers to drink an espresso that gives him the energy while going on comedy tours or working on sets.

    RELATED: 10 Times Jerry Seinfeld Became Unlikeable With The Fans

    2 David Letterman

    David Letterman has famously quoted, "If it wasn’t for coffee, I wouldn’t have a personality." Letterman is best known as the talk-show host of The Late Show With David Letterman and interacts with guests while consuming coffee. While he usually preferred to drink decaf, he didn’t hesitate to add caffeine sometimes.

    1 Sofia Vergara

    Growing up in a South American family, Sofia Vergara had known a lot about coffee and flavors from a young age. The actress was only seven years old when she began drinking espresso like the adults in her family, and she worked as a barista in college. Vergara likes her coffee strong and black without sugar. She drinks a cup of coffee before working out and several cups throughout until the afternoon. She stops her intake post-afternoon from getting a good night’s sleep.

    Other celebrities with coffee obsession include Jackie Chan, Gigi Hadid, and Sienna Miller. Coffee has become a part of the culture that turns to caffeine while struggling with addiction or needing a boost of energy to get through a tough day. Consuming coffee keeps them going and helps them get a fresh start every morning.
