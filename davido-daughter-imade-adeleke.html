    Davido daughter Imade Adeleke is an Instagram influencer and a child model. She is seven years old and known for being Davido’s daughter. 

    She was born to Sophia Momodu and Davido on November 14, 2015. She is Davido’s first and oldest daughter.

    Imade unquestionably gained notice for being the daughter of the famous Nigerian musician and has continued to do so.

    Despite having a demanding schedule as a musician, Davido never hesitates to assume proper parental duties. Imade and his other children experience him as a constant father.

    Imade recently suffered the loss of her half-brother, Ifeanyi. According to police, her brother died drowning in the singer’s swimming pool at home.

    The family has not addressed this publicly yet.

    Overview Of Her Life

    Imade is presently enrolled in basic one at Park View International School in Ikoyi, Lagos, after completing her pre-primary schooling. 

    In June 2018, she received her kindergarten diploma. 

    Imade started her own organic hair care product line in 2019 when she was just four years old, and Davido wasted no time in announcing the launch of her daughter’s enterprise, Aurora by Imade, on his Instagram page.

    This significant step marked Imade’s debut in the fashion and cosmetics industries. She is a well-known figure on Instagram with a sizable following.

    Sophia Momodu, Imade’s mom is a fashion connoisseur. Sophia is a brand influencer, entrepreneur, philanthropist, and adviser for the fashion industry. 

    She serves as the creative director and CEO of The Sophia Momodu Brand (TSMB). She has thousands of followers and subscribers on YouTube and is a well-known figure online.

    David Adedeji Adeleke is a Nigerian singer, songwriter, and record producer better known by his stage name Davido. His music fuses worldwide pop with traditional African themes.

    Imade Parents first met in 2013, and soon after, they welcomed a daughter. Davido was only 21 years old at the time, while Sophia was in her late 20s. 

    Davido ended his relationship with Sophia abruptly shortly after the birth of Imade. 

    She has developed into one of the wealthiest children of Nigerian celebrities. Imade Adeleke, only seven years old, has already signed on to serve as the brand ambassador for a leading Nigerian company.

    Popular Instagram starlet Aurora Imade Adeleke has an estimated net worth of around $1 million.

    The Custody War

    Drama broke out seven months later when Davido forcibly took custody of Imade in a court case involving Sophia’s uncle Dele Momodu.

    On several occasions, Sophia and Davido engaged in open altercations.

    It even reached the point where Davido’s family planned to remove the youngster from the country without her mother’s permission. 

    However, they were unable to do so because they were subjected to airport restrictions because of child trafficking.

    Sophia and Davido eventually reconciled their disagreements as they co-parented their daughter.

    Davido does his best to be a good parent by making periodic visits even though Sophia has custody of their baby now. 

    According to reports, Sophia resides with their daughter, Imade, in Davido’s mansion in Banana Isle.
