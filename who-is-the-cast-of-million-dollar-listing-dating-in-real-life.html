    Since its first season in 2006, the 'Million Dollar Listing' cast has been gluing fans to the couch thanks to some stunning properties and some behind-the-scenes drama and romances.

    Bravo's reality TV show, whose original version is based in Los Angeles, has spawned three other series, set in Miami, New York and San Francisco. Well before the ladies of 'Selling Sunset' took the real estate world by storm, the 'Million Dollar Listing' gang had put their professional and personal lives in front of the cameras, their rows and relationships being as intriguing as some of the most beautiful high-end properties up for sale.

    Nearly 15 years after the show premiered, where are the 'Million Dollar Listing' cast members, and who are they dating today?

    10 'Million Dollar Listing: Los Angeles' Star Josh Flagg Is Very Much In Love With His New Beau

    Following his split from Bobby Boyd, Beverly Hills real estate agent Josh Flagg has started seeing someone new and it looks like their blossoming relationship is very much on track to become something special.

    Flagg shared pictures with boyfriend and fellow realtor Andrew Beyer not long after separating from Boyd.

    Related: The Cast Of Bravo's 'Million Dollar Listing' Ranked By Net Worth

    "It came a couple of weeks after Bobby and I split," Flagg told 'E! News' in March, a month before Beyer first appeared on his Instagram.

    "We went on a date and things went from there. We found that we really got along well and liked each other a lot."

    9 LA's Josh Altman Has Adorable Date Nights With "His Queen," Wife Heather

    Star of 'Million Dollar Listing: Los Angeles' Josh Altman has been married to Heather Altman (née Bilyeu) since 2016.

    The couple met on the show, Heather also being a real estate agent. They started dating in 2010 with Josh popping the question in 2013. The pair wedded three years later and welcomed two children, son Ace and daughter Lexi.

    On his Instagram page, Josh often shares a glimpse of his tender moments with "my queen" Heather, including a recent picture of them on the way to have a special date night.

    8 Tracy Tutor Is Smitten With Boyfriend Erik Anderson

    'Million Dollar Listing: Los Angeles' star Tracy Tutor has found happiness with her new partner and personal trainer Erik Anderson.

    The pair have been dating since 2020. It was apparently one of Tracy's 'Million Dollar Listing' co-stars who put them together: Fredrik Heklund.

    Tracy's colleague commented the couple's first Instagram post in July 2020 with a cheeky "Don’t forget who put you together," to which Tracy replied, "You did boo."

    Tracy was previously married to Jason Maltas, and the two are co-parenting teen daughters Juliet and Scarlett.

    7 David Parnes Has Just Welcomed A Second Child With Wife Adrian

    Hailing from the UK, luxury real estate agent and former 'MDLLA' star David Parnes did find love in the States, where he met his wife Adrian Abnosi Parnes.

    Related: James Harris and David Parnes Leaving 'Million Dollar Listing' After Seven Seasons

    The pair married in France in 2017 and had their first child, India, the following year.

    As per their Instagram, They have recently welcomed their second daughter, Lily Jane.

    6 'Million Dollar Listing' Star Fredrik Eklund Is Happily Married To Derek Kaplan

    Alongside playing Cupid for his co-star Tracy Tutor, Fredrik Eklund has a very happy personal life.

    The real estate agent, who has appeared on both 'Million Dollar Listing: Los Angeles' and 'Million Dollar Listing: New York,' has been married to Derek Kaplan since 2013.

    The couple welcomed their twins Freddy and Milla in 2017 and reside in New York.

    5 'Million Dollar Listing: LA' Cast Member Madison Hildebrand Is Reportedly Single

    Former 'Million Dollar Listing: Los Angeles' star Madison Hildebrand is currently enjoying the single life.

    The real estate agent was previously in a relationship with Cody Keltz. The two had been dating for about six months before season 10 started in 2017, with Keltz even appearing on the show. In one of the episodes, Madison asked Cody to move in with him.

    However, they broke up a few months later, in 2018. On Valentine's Day that year, Madison announced he was back on the market.

    4 James Harris And Wife Valeria Are Going Strong After 12 Years Of Marriage

    The British realtor, who has said goodbye to 'Million Dollar Listing: Los Angeles' earlier this year with partner David Parnes, has recently celebrated his 12th wedding anniversary with wife Valeria.

    The two met when Valeria was waiting tables while attending business school, with James often eating at the restaurant where she worked.

    On their 12th anniversary, celebrated in May this year, Harris shared pictures of him and Valeria with their kids, including a sweet message for his wife: "You are hands down my biggest accomplishment in life."

    3 'Million Dollar Listing: New York' Realtor Kirsten Jordan Has Been Married For 11 Years

    Another anniversary for another 'Million Dollar Listing' star.

    Kirsten Jordan from 'Million Dollar Listing: New York,' has recently marked her 11-year wedding anniversary with real estate developer Stefano Farsura in a sweet Instagram post.

    Related: 'Million Dollar Listing New York': Does Kirsten Jordan Get Along With The Other Brokers?

    "Nothing like a weekend in Portofino to reset and celebrate 11 years with my @stefano_farsura," she captioned a snap of them all glammed up for a wedding in Italy last week.

    2 Samantha DeBianchi From 'Million Dollar Listing: Miami' Is Expecting Her Second Child With Husband

    Sam DeBianchi LaViola, star of the short-lived 'Million Dollar Listing: Miami,' is currently pregnant with her second child.

    She and husband Anthony LaViola tied the knot in 2019. They met two years prior when DeBianchi lived next door to one of the homes that LaViola owned and rented on Airbnb.

    They welcomed their first child, Domenic, in 2020 and are gearing up to meet baby number two.

    1 'Million Dollar Listing: San Francisco' Cast Member Roh Habibi Has Been With His Wife For 16 Years

    His show 'Million Dollar Listing: San Francisco' may have been canceled after just one season back in 2015, but real estate agent Roh Habibi has had way more long-lasting luck in his personal life.

    The realtor is married to Shugufa, his wife of 16 years, who occasionally appears on his Instagram page.

    On Mother's Day this year, Roh shared a sweet message for his wife and mother of his kids.

    "Grateful for every minute with you, and how amazing you are with our babies," he wrote.

    'Million Dollar Listing: Los Angeles' and 'Million Dollar Listing: New York' air on Bravo.
