    Mayim Bialik's Recent Departure From 'Jeopardy!'

    It's fitting that Mayim Bialik would look back on her past work now that a chapter of her professional life is ending. In December 2023, it was announced that she would be stepping down as host of Jeopardy! and that Ken Jennings would become the only presenter.
