    American film and television actor, Michael Clarke Duncan had an estimated net worth of $18 million. Michael Clarke Duncan was best known for his role in The Green Mile. He was nominated for an Academy Award and a Golden Globe. Duncan also appeared in movies like Armageddon, The Whole Nine Yards, Daredevil and voice acting roles in Brother Bear and Delgo.

    Duncan started his career as security in Los Angeles and went on to appear in some in commercials. He worked as a bodyguard for stars like Will Smith, Martin Lawrence, Jamie Foxx, LL Cool J, and Notorious B.I.G. His his breakout came in 1999 with a role as John Coffey in The Green Mile. He died on September 3, 2012, nearly two months after suffering a heart attack.
