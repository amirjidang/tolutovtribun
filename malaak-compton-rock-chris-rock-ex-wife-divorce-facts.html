    As anyone who pays any attention to celebrity gossip will no doubt be able to attest, famous people start dating one another and break up all the time. In fact, within the first few months of 2020, there already were a slew of celebrity relationships that ended. Given the fact that most regular people tend to think of celebrities as being somehow different than the rest of us, it can be too easy to think of those breakups as juicy gossip.

    In reality, when celebrity couples split, whether their partner is famous or not, it likely is a traumatic experience for everyone involved. On top of that, some of those celebrity relationships come to an end for really serious reasons. Of course, since most stars are very aware that their careers depend on their fans liking them, they tend to either keep the reasons for their breakups private or blame their former partner.

    Remarkably enough, since Chris Rock and his wife of many years broke up in 2016, the comedian has been extremely open about why they split up. More importantly, during interviews, he has made it very clear that he deserves the blame for the way things played out. As for the exact things Rock has said about his divorce, you’ll need to read on to get a complete picture.

    Related: Chris Rock And Howard Stern Got Revenge On Their High School Bullies

    An Impressive Career

    In Hollywood, it can be extremely difficult for anyone to have a lasting career either as an actor or as a comedian. For that reason, there is no doubt that Chris Rock deserves a lot of credit since he has found long-lasting success in both arenas.

    Introduced to the masses when he began starring in Saturday Night Live, Chris Rock managed to become a major movie star once he left that show behind. Most memorably, Rock starred in the Madagascar and Grown Ups franchises on top of stand-alone movies like Down to Earth and The Longest Yard. After years of stardom, Rock is in the midst of taking his acting career in a new direction at the time of this writing. Currently starring in the fourth season of TV’s Fargo, Rock has said that the show has given him the best role of his career and he is right.

    Related: Chris Rock Reveals Why He's So Unapproachable

    Speaking Out

    As a comedian, it is Chris Rock’s job to see the funny side of life and point it out for the people that show up to see him perform. For that reason, one year after Rock’s 2016 divorce was finalized, he started to address the situation in front of audiences and playing it up for laughs. For example, during one of Rock’s performances that was attended by an interviewer for Rolling Stone, Rock said he was “a piece of sh**” during his marriage.

    Elaborating on what made him a bad husband during that same comedy show, Chris Rock spoke about the many times he was unfaithful to his wife. Talking about three women he slept with while married, according to that Rolling Stone interviewer, Rock said one was famous, another one was “semifamous, and the last one worked retail. Of course, Rock is far from the first celebrity who has cheated but, amazingly, he is willing to talk about his infidelity in great detail. Aware of how awful he’d been during his marriage, Rock then went on to urge the audience to hold on tightly to the people they love before transitioning into a joke.

    Continuing to talk about his divorce on stage in the months that followed that Rolling Stones profile, during his Netflix special Chris Rock: Tamborine, he was very open once again. This time speaking about more than just his infidelities, Rock said that his marriage ended in part because he didn’t listen to his wife nearly enough during their time together.

    Moving On

    Since Chris Rock and his ex-wife put an end to their romantic relationship, they have continued to be a big part of one another’s lives. After all, the couple had two daughters together during their many years together. Based on how Rock has talked about his divorce in public, you may assume that the exes working out a custody plan would have been pretty cut and dry. However, that certainly wasn’t the case.

    According to documents that Chris Rock filed during the process of his divorce, at one point his ex-wife was keeping their two kids away from him. Of course, it should go without saying that anyone would find it very difficult to suddenly not be able to see the people you love most in the world.

    According to a statement that Rock’s ex-wife put out through her lawyers; she was “disappointed that Chris has accused her of keeping their children from him, which he knows is untrue and can be so easily disproved." No matter how things were playing out behind the scenes at that time, from all accounts it seems like Chris and his ex have since worked out a co-parenting plan.
