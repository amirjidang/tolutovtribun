    20 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Kesha appears to be continuing her streak of health and happiness into 2015. The “TiK ToK” singer, who spent time in rehab for an eating disorder in February 2014, displayed a fit, toned figure while on the beach with boyfriend Brad Ashenfelter in Brazil on Monday, Jan. 26.

    Wearing a black one-piece swimsuit, the songstress, who is engaged in a nasty legal battle with producer Dr. Luke, looked happier than ever to be in Brazil, where she’s currently performing.

    During an interview with Ryan Seacrest this past June, the “Timber” singer gushed about her boyfriend just three months after leaving rehab.

    “He’s really, a really sweet human being,” the 27-year-old songstress said on-air. “I’m very happy to have him in my life…I’m really happy with my beard.”
