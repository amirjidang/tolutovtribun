    11 Bella Thorne And Kenton Duty (2010)

    Bella Thorne's relationship with Kenton Duty was about as "puppy love" as it gets. The two starred together on Disney Channel's Shake It Up and after getting close off-screen, they dated for a couple of months before breaking up. It never really became anything serious and was more fun than anything.

    10 Bella Thorne And Tristan Klier (2011)

    Tristan Klier was the first long-term relationship Bella Thorne ever had, and they managed to last three years as a couple. They became an item back in 2011, and he was not famous by any means. Some might assume that her fame came between them as he was a regular kid while she was a rising star on social media. According to Klier, their breakup was likely caused because he "wasn't always the most appreciative boyfriend."

    9 Bella Thorne And Brandon Lee (2015)

    Brandon Lee is the son of Pamela Anderson and Tommy Lee, and he briefly dated Bella Thorne back in 2015. They went to a few parties together and were seen flirting on Twitter, but it didn't last long for the pair. He was a rebound for her after her breakup with her ex-boyfriend, Tristan Klier.

    8 Bella Thorne And Ryan Nassif (2015)

    Ryan Nassif is the son of a Hollywood talent agent and the nephew of a former surgeon on the Real Housewives Of Beverly Hills, Paul Nassif. He was seen with Bella Thorne at a Lakers basketball game back in 2015 just a few days after she shared her relationship with Brandon Lee with the world. It was hard to deny that things were over with Lee after sharing a photographed kiss with Nassif at the game.

    7 Bella Thorne And Gregg Sulkin (2015)

    Bella Thorne's relationship with Gregg Sulkin was one of her notable long-term relationships, but they only lasted a year before calling it quits. The pair started out as friends before turning it into something more romantic.

    6 Bella Thorne And Bella Pendergast (2016)

    Bella Pendergast was originally dating Bella Thorne's brother, Remy before the pair made it official in 2016. It followed her split with Gregg Sulkin and was the first time that Thorne had openly declared herself a bisexual.

    Pendergast had been friends with Thorne for years and was a college student at the time of their relationship. However, some questioned whether or not it was real as Pendergast's own Instagram showed multiple pictures of her with a boy, and the captions made it seem like she and the boy were more than just friends.

    5 Bella Thorne And Tyler Posey (2016)

    Tyler Posey is an actor who was made famous after his role on a show called Teen Wolf, and he began dating Bella Thorne just a few weeks after she came out as bisexual. They loved to share their romance on social media with plenty of poolside pictures that had fans obsessed with them as an item. All good things come to an end though, as the pair went through a difficult breakup that left Thorne looking for love elsewhere.

    4 Bella Thorne And Charlie Puth (2016)

    Bella Thorne swears that her and Charlie Puth were never an item, but the pictures of them together and the feud that resulted make this hard to believe. They were spotted showing quite a bit of PDA at Miami Beach back in 2016 after her breakup from Posey.

    3 Bella Thorne, Mod Sun And Tana Mongeau (2018)

    Bella Thorne's dating life took quite the turn as she began dating both Mod Sun, a rapper, and a YouTuber named Tana Mongeau. She was originally in an open relationship with Mod Sun and dated Mongeau at the same time.

    2 Bella Thorne And Alex Martini (2019)

    Alex Martini is a set production assistant who has apparently been dating Bella Thorne while she has been with Benjamin Mascolo. It was even given the seal of approval by her current boyfriend, as well as her ex-girlfriend, Tana Mongeau. She was called 'camera shy' by Thorne on social media. Bella Thorne has been known for living a polyamorous lifestyle, though she's admitted it can be hard to find balance. In an interview with Cosmopolitan, Bella stated she was only dating Benjamin, which lead fans to believe she and Alex split sometime after October 2019.

    1 Bella Thorne And Benjamin Mascolo (2019)

    Benjamin Mascolo, an Italian singer, began dating Bella Thorne in 2019, and she immediately took to social media to share how in love she was with him. They gush about one another to the world constantly. In March 2021, Mascolo popped the question, asking Bella Thorne to marry him, and of course, she said, "yes!" The couple, who star in an upcoming movie together, have hopes of marrying in summer of 2022 and want to hold a double celebration for their wedding -- on in Italy and on in the U.S.

    "It's going to be, we think that'd be next year, next summer. We hope between June, July, August. The truth is, we have so much work, we want to find the time to enjoy the wedding. We don't want to rush it," Benjamin Mascolo explained, according to ET. "We don't want it to be between jobs and projects because it's such an important moment of our life because it has to be the best days of our life. And it's already stressful to organize a big wedding like we want to do, so that's already enough on our plate."
