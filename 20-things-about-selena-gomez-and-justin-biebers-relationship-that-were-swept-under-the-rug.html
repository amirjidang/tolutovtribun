    Looking at Justin Bieber through the lens of his teen idol status, it's easy to believe The Biebs understands a thing or two about being an ultimate fantasy boyfriend! For over a decade, Justin has serenaded millions of gals with his lyrics adorned with promises of puppy love. Posters featuring his likeness have adorned countless bedroom walls, and his signature baby-faced look won over many hearts back in the day!

    Justin's real-life relationship with winning over women has involved the breaking of some hearts; a dose of reality and a sign our JB has grown up a bit. Long before Justin wanted the world to know how much he loves his missus, he won (and broke) the heart of Selena Gomez, the Disney darling who evolved into the mature songstress who has let fans in on a tidbit about her and Mr. Bieber's whirlwind romance. Here are 20 additional tidbits!

    20 Selena Went 'Numb' Over The Biebs

    Selena's bop about her longtime love spilled the tea within the first minute; she says, "In two months you replaced us." This slice of autobiographical pie was brought to us by her ex's fiercely fast timing between the end of the 'Jelena' relationship and the beginning of his relationship with his eventual bride, Hailey Baldwin.

    Selena revealed she felt "numb" upon learning of their relationship!

    19 Drama With Justin Made Selena Feel 'Stupid'

    Selena's vulnerability through her lyrics isn't new. Even in the middle of her longtime relationship with Justin, Selena stepped into the recording studio and released, "The Heart Wants What It Wants," a ballad she's performed in public many times, where she's had to relive her confusion and sadness over the state of their relationship.

    The recording process was very intimate; Selena's mic was hidden!

    18 Their First Date Involved Passion Over Pancakes

    It's hard to believe, but there was a time when these wildly successful celebrities were just two teens in love!

    Remember those dates from teenhood full of awkwardness, but still full of sweet puppy love? Selena and Justin's first date was a classic date you'd go on as a young couple; the duo had breakfast together and bonded over pancakes. How syrupy sweet!

    17 Selena Made Justin Shed Some Tears

    Relationships can be tough and it's rare for couples to speak about the difficult moments. Selena didn't share this sentiment; when she and Justin split for the first time, Selena was ready to spill!

    Selena's interview with David Letterman began as a simple interview, but ended as revealing tell-all about their breakup! David makes a joke about making Justin cry, and so does Selena!

    16 Selena Did This Super Sketchy Activity After Their First Breakup

    Between the breakups and makeups between Selena and Justin, there was plenty of room for Selena to do what she needed in order to step away from the Biebs!

    During a Bieber off-season, Selena went to blow off steam at a shooting range, and she captured the event in a since-deleted post on social media. Selena wasn't specific, but timelines show it was a Bieber-blowout.

    15 The Duo Had A Rocky Relationship With Vacations

    Vacations with your beau are supposed to be full of potential, and let's be real, downright romantic! Unfortunately for Selena and Justin, they seemed to struggle when they took the time for some relaxation!

    Selena and Justin's efforts to have some private time together were often cut short when the duo would have arguments that weren't exactly serene. Once, Selena flew home early!

    14 Justin Was Forced To Be 'Kandid' Over A Jenner

    Justin Bieber may have a few female leads in his life, but we almost never hear about the "supporting" cast members in his realm!

    The woman who sits between Selena and Hailey Baldwin is a woman we're all familiar with: Kylie Jenner.

    A flame was lit between Selena and the famous friends when she discovered "photos of Kylie on his phone," according to Cosmo. Eek!

    13 Orlando Bloom Wasn't Having Justin's Chaos Over Selena

    Even though Selena may have felt super sad during her break from Justin, she wasted no time meeting and spending time with one of the hottest men in Hollywood!

    Selena spent a bit of time with Orlando Bloom during a Bieber off-season! Orlando, however, had no time to spend agonizing over Selena's former beau's shenanigans. Biebs and Bloom once had a rough encounter featuring fists!

    12 Another Woman In Justin's Life Wasn't Over 'Jelena'

    One of the surefire signs you're succeeding in a relationship is if you've got the stamp of approval from your significant other's parents! It's a different story, however, when your S/O's mother is still carrying a torch for you, even after your relationship's flame burns out!

    Justin's mother, Patti, was tight enough with Selena, despite her son's rocky relationship, even exchanging messages with Selena online!

    11 Justin's Girl Made Him Stop Listening To Music

    Sorry, not sorry, JB!

    Selena did exactly what she felt she needed to do in order to get over her on-again, off-again beau; she dove back into the dating pool! Her first public relationship after Justin was a ten-month courtship with The Weeknd.

    Justin surely couldn't avoid The Weeknd back in 2017; his songs were all over the radio, much to Justin's chagrin!

    10 Hockey Was Close To Their Hearts

    At a point when Selena and Justin's relationship was heating up again after a hiatus, the pair discovered they enjoyed partaking in an activity a bit chillier than their relationship status at the time.

    Selena embraced one of her boyfriend's passionate and personal pastimes: hockey! When 'Jelena' reunited (yet again) in 2017, they were photographed snuggling at the ice rink!

    9 Their Families Weren't Having Fun With Their Relationship

    If 'Jelena's' emotional rollercoaster of a relationship was hard for fans to keep tabs on, imagine how difficult it may have felt to those closest to the couple!

    Selena's relationship with her mother has been known to be just as sporadic as her relationship with Justin. When her mother Mandy was questioned about her daughter's reconciliation with Justin, Mama had very few positive things to say.

    8 Justin Questionably Handled A Significant Event In Selena's Life

    Justin may not have been Selena's forever, but she has plenty of "forevers" in her life. Her relationship with her fans has certainly outlasted her relationship with The Biebs and her vow to always be transparent may have something to do with it.

    Sel shared an incredibly intimate photo with her fans revealing she had a kidney transplant. Justin, however, seemed to miss the memo.

    7 Selena Loved To Get Cryptic

    What fun would it be if our favorite celebs revealed every bit of their personal lives, leaving no evidence for fans to uncover? None, right?

    Selena certainly kept as tight-lipped as possible while penning songs about her Bieber beau. She isn't really the type to sing and tell, but fans have kept their ears open and were ready to analyze any new lyrics for tidbits!

    6 Selena's Beau Had To Be Okay With Her Ex-Boyfriend

    Despite the fact that Selena and Justin found new partners during their off-season, it doesn't mean Selena was necessarily ready to let her ex-boyfriend go. What exactly was your purpose, Selena?

    Her leftover feelings for Justin lingered a bit when Selena began dating the Weeknd. According to Vogue Australia, the status of their relationship was quiet for a bit as she had "friendly" breakfasts with Justin.

    5 The Pair Moved In Together Extremely Early

    Wow, put on the brakes!

    It goes without saying Justin and Selena's relationship was intense at times. Now that we know this fact that the former couple would understandably want to sweep under the rug, we can see why their love was so strong!

    According to Cosmopolitan, Justin revealed to Complex, "I moved in with my girlfriend when I was 18." Serious, much?

    4 Justin Even Bought Sel A Sparkler

    Way before Justin was making headlines for presenting his sudden bride-to-be, Hailey Baldwin, with an engagement ring, he had some practice. There's no doubt both Justin and Hailey would want the fact that he once bought a ring for his former flame to be swept under the rug!

    Yes, that's right; Justin bought some bling for Selena.

    3 Selena Was Practical About Justin's Joking Ways

    There are lots of aspects of being in the public eye that a celebrity may not want to reveal, like the pressure to grow up fast and never act your actual age. Justin Bieber was a teen idol, but his career and fame left him little time to act his age, so he had to seize any moment he could!

    He often enjoyed pranking Selena.

    2 Mamas Made It Happen

    Despite Selena's mother's later reluctance for the young lovers to reunite, she was all about a potential relationship between her daughter and the floppy-haired "Baby" singer.

    Reminiscent of a classic love story, the saga of 'Jelena' began with a phone call between two moms, according to Teen Vogue. Pattie and Mandy were eager to see where their young entertainers could go!

    1 Bieber Was Like A 'Brother' To Her

    Don't toy with us, Selena!

    Buzz surrounding a potential relationship with Justin Bieber pretty much began immediately when the youngins were first spotted out together, and, as the pair grew more comfortable together, they began gradually taking their relationship public. Selena wasn't quite ready to spill the beans during an interview with Ellen DeGeneres, where she comically referred to Justin as her "little brother!"
