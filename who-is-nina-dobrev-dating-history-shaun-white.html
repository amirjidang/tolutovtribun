Even before that, she starred in the Canadian teen TV series Degrassi playing the role of a teenager who was raising a baby alongside Drake.

    Nina Dobrev has been very open about her friendships and relationships over the years. Most people know that she is close with Kat Graham, Candice Accola, and other popular actresses. Nina Dobrev's dating history is a little more interesting and complex than that though! These are ten guys she has been romantically linked to.

    Updated on December 21, 2021, by Michael Chaar: Nina Dobrev hasn't been shy about her love life! The actress's first major public romance was with Vampire Diaries co-star, Ian Somerhalder. Since then, fans have wondered who Nina Dobrev is dating, and turns out she's been romantically linked to a few men including Glenn Powell, Derek Hough, and Liam Hemsworth, however, it is pro snowboarder, Shaun White that has her heart today. The duo have been dating since the pandemic began. Reports claim that Shaun is expected to propose to Nina any day now and fans couldn't be happier!

    10 Nina Dobrev's First Public Romance

    One of Nina Dobrev's longest-lasting relationships in history was her relationship with Ben Hollingsworth. They dated from 2006 to 2009. This was before she landed her leading role on The Vampire Diaries.

    Despite the fact that both of them never publicly spoke out about their relationship, they were photographed spending time together for three consecutive years and he was a guest at her 21st birthday party which pretty much says it all. Today, Ben is not only married to Nila Billingsworth, but the duo shares two kids together!

    9 Nina Dobrev Dated Ian Somerhalder Betwee 2010 - 2013

    Nina Dobrev’s relationship with Ian Somerhalder developed on the set of The Vampire Diaries. She played the human teenage girl and he played the handsome, brooding vampire. On camera, fans were rooting for the relationship of their fictional characters to work out.

    In real life, the relationship that they were going through was actually falling apart. Ian Somerhalder ended up getting into a relationship with another actress who has played a vampire in the past… Nikki Reed (Twilight.) Because of all of this, Nina decided to part ways with The Vampire Diaries early. While the duo didn't work out, Ian has since moved on and married Nikki, with whom he shares a 4-year-old daughter, Bodhi.

    8 Nina Dobrev's Relationship With Derek Hough

    Derek Hough is another ex-boyfriend of Nina Dobrev. The reason they met? He is Julianne Hough’s older brother— and Nina and Julianne are considered best friends! It seems like this would be a match made in heaven but unfortunately, it didn’t work.

    Things for this couple started falling apart after six weeks of dating.

    They knew pretty early on that it wasn’t going to work out and they decided to call it quits. This breakup did not impact her friendship with Julianne. The pro dancer is now in a relationship with Hayley Erbert, and the pressure to propose is definitely on!

    They were spotted in New Orleans sharing a kiss at a karaoke bar in June 2014. This is the type of couple that would’ve instantly gotten labeled a “power couple." James is reportedly dating musician, Edei, who is 15 years James' junior.

    Orlando Bloom is happily in love with Katy Perry these days but back in 2014 he was spotted getting super cozy with Nina Dobrev.
