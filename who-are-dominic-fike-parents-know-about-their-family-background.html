    Dominic Fike parents are Dave and Jessica Fike-McCann. Dominic Fike grew up alongside three siblings namely, Alex, Apollonia and Sean.

    The musician was left on his own, often without any economic and emotional support. He grew up listening to rap songs in Naples, a small town in Southern Florida.

    Recently, Dominic released a song titled Dancing in the Courthouse on Youtube. He showed his gratitude toward Jessica for her support in the description.

    The song is inspired by his upbringing in Florida and his struggle with the law at a young age. After the song got released, he shared some memories from childhood with his younger brother Alex.

    The single mother gave birth to Fike at a young age and got separated from her former partner. She then remarried and gave motherhood to her stepson named Sean Christian McCann.

    In an interview with Id, Dominic shared his father Dave taught him some chords to play guitar. The father-son duo would make music videos on GarageBand.

    They even made a Thanksgiving and showed the family where he rapped, and Alex did the singing part. Their love for music comes from their dad.

    Family Relationships

    Dominic Fike family includes his parents and three siblings. Dominic has musically-included siblings Sean and ALX.

    They faced a lot of racism growing up in Naples. They were surrounded by all the rich people around.

    Dominic Fike ethnicity is of Filipino and African-American descent. Since he has been staying in the United States since he was a child, he holds American nationality.

    Mom

    Dominic Fike mother Jessica Fike-McCann is the office manager at Southwest Florida Ankle & Foot Care. She has been working for the company since August 2006.

    Jessica originally hails from Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. She attended Fort Myers High School from 1999 to 2003.

    Dad

    Dominic Fike dad Dave left him when his brother Alex was in middle school. He also has his hands in music composing and singing.

    As Per ID, Dave and Jessica argued one day and made Dave move out. He took his razor scooter and left the house.

    Before going, he said, “You know, I’m not leaving you guys; I’m leaving your mom.”

    Dominic added the he left the house with no child support. They tried to reach him but they never responded.

    Brother And Sister

    Dominic Fike siblings are Sean, Alex and Apollonia. He grew up as a middle child.

    Alex Fike

    Dominic Fike brother Alex Fike aka ALX is a musician from Naples. The singer self-declared himself as a horny-pop recording artist.

    The unconventional singer attended Gulfview Middle School.

    He inspired Dom to ink the face tattoo of an Apple logo. Alex has a horny tattoo on his arm. He has inked Jessica’s name on his left arm.

    Fike has released some music including PPS, Run Out, Right Guy and Out of the Blur. His debut song was Hungover.

    The musician recently welcomed a baby boy named Hunny with his partner Madison Mitchell. He was born on 16 September 2021.

    Sean Christian McCann

    Dominic stepbrother Sean Christian McCann is the CEO of Backhouse Music. He is also a recording artist, music business consultant, and songwriter.
    The entrepreneur has worked for almost a decade as an independent professional artist. He founded  Backhouse Music in 2018 to promote emerging and established artists.

    According to his LinkedIn, he attended Edison State Community College. He went to Naples High School.

    Apollonia Fike

    Dominic Fike sister Apollonia Fike aka Apple is younger than him.

    In 2019, the musician shared with Complex that his apple tattoo wasn’t any honor to the brand but a heartfelt tribute to her. The ink reminds him of what he has to do and what he is working for.

    The following year, he released a song titled Cancel Me on Youtube. He featured Apollonia playing around the beach with him and having fun together.
