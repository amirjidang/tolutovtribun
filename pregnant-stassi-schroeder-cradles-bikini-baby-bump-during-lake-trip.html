    Pregnant Stassi Schroeder Cradles Bikini Baby Bump During Lake Trip

    Fun in the sun! Pregnant Stassi Schroeder enjoyed a day at the lake with her fianc, Beau Clark, on Sunday, August 30. My sweets, the commercial casting director, 40, captioned an Instagram Story photo of the Vanderpump Rules alum, 32, cradling her bare baby bump in a navy blue bikini.

    18 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Fun in the sun! Pregnant Stassi Schroeder enjoyed a day at the lake with her fiancé, Beau Clark, on Sunday, August 30.

    “My sweets,” the commercial casting director, 40, captioned an Instagram Story photo of the Vanderpump Rules alum, 32, cradling her bare baby bump in a navy blue bikini.

    The Florida native went on to document their fun-filled day, from attempting to float to tanning.

    Us Weekly broke the news in June that Schroeder is pregnant with her and Clark’s first child. The pair confirmed the news later that same week, announcing the sex of their baby-to-be.

    “We’re having a baby girl,” the former reality star wrote via Instagram at the time.

    Earlier this month, the Next Level Basic author’s former costar Katie Maloney gushed to Us exclusively about what a “caring mom” Schroeder will be.

    “She has such a maternal sort of aspect of her,” the Utah native, 33, explained at the time. “She’s really caring and likes to care for people and wants to just have a little mini her. I’m like ‘Oh, my gosh, she’s going to be a little devil.’ … [She’s] already got, like, a full year’s wardrobe

    Maloney added that the former “Straight Up With Stassi” podcast host will make a “good” parenting pair with Clark, saying, “Stassi definitely has more of the feminine energy and Beau’s a little more rambunctious, so it will be interesting to see like who she takes after more.”

    Schroeder previously opened up about her plans to become a mom, exclusively telling Us in June 2019 that she was “ready to have a baby.”
