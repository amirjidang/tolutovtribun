    Reflecting on her career, Pauley commenced her career in the field of advertising. Later, she started to book commercials and further moved to Los Angeles to pursue a career in acting. Apart from that, Perrette took the first step in the field of acting by making guest appearances in prominent shows.

    Moreover, in the year 2011, she landed her current role as a forensic scientist Abby Sciuto in NCIS, a series based on the Naval Criminal Investigative Service. Besides this, she has also made her appearance on two episodes of JAG in the year 2003. In fact, this small role brought her to the attention of many people.

    Other than this, she also got the highest Q Score for any actor on the US prime time show in the year 2011. Later, she went to appear in movies like ‘The Ring’ and Almost Famous. Besides being an actress, Pauley is a poet, writer, photographer, and music lover. Other than this, she had one of the top Q Scores which landed her alongside Morgan Freeman and Tom Hanks.

    Moreover, she became the only woman to make it to the top 10. In the same year, she also started doing television commercials for Expedia.com. Additionally, she also has sung several songs. In fact, her song, Fear features in the NCIS: The Official TV Soundtrack. Similarly, she has also sung in a Los-Angeles based band Lo-Ball.

    However, on June 7th, 2019, she made an announcement that she has left NCIS due to an on-set dog bite and alleged multiple physical assaults. Perrette announced her retirement from acting on July 7, 2020.

    Apart from her acting career, Pauley has co-written a book titled Donna Bell’s Bake Shop: Recipes and Stories of Family, Friends, and Food. The book describes the story of how her two best friends and Pauley started a Southern-style bake shop in Manhattan.

    Talking about her personal life, Pauley tied the sacred knot of marriage with actor and musician Coyote Shivers in the year 1998. However, their relation couldn’t last long due to some personal issues and broke up in the year 2004.

    Later, she got engaged with a cameraman Michael Bosman. But the duo was not legally married but was together from 2005 to 2009. Furthermore, in the year 2011, Perrette made an announcement of her engagement to Thomas Arklie, former British Royal Marine. Moreover, she doesn’t have any children from any of her relationships.
